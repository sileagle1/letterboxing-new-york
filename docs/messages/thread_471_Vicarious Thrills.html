Sorry about the lateness- I did mean to chime in earlier and thank Lockwench for putting together a very fun event! I had a great time at DSSun--meeting everyone, eating (wow, there are a lot of great letterboxing cooks out there!)-- and of course searching for stamps, exchanging, etc. LW obviously put in so much work into making this a fun event- Thank You! :o) The only bummers were that a) Out in the wind, I could not get good stamp images on any of my stampings, LOL- drat that winter wind, LOL! ;o) and b) Someone stole the massive pink carving block out of the Carving Basket that Seeker won- it disappeared sometime between the time she won it and the time she left. :o< I guess what that means is ... anyone who attended DSSun should check their purses and backpacks (if they haven't already) because SOMEONE who attended had "sticky fingers" and was on the prowl. That's just rotten! Grrr. ~:o{

At any rate... Lockwench thank you! It must have taken a lot of time and effort to put something like this together and we appreciate it! A nice weekend break from the snow and cold!
