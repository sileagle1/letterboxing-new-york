Hi fellow NY boxers ... OK, well, I'm in MA, but I am a native NYer!

I'll be heading home (Canandaigua area) for the Christmas holiday and
was thinking about maybe placing a box or two while I'm there. I've
wanted to place a number of boxes in the Finger Lakes since long
before many of you NY boxers were boxers, but never really got around
to it. Now that there are a growing number of NY boxers in the western
NY area, I'm quickly losing places to plant!

But I'm determined to place a few never-the-less, and the week between
Christmas and New Years is the perfect time to do it.

I have a number of ideas, but here's the rub -- I'm awful at carving!
Even if boxers don't mind poorly carved stamps, I know that the best
and most hunted boxes out there have nice art to reward the finder.
Besides, I don't really enjoy carving all that much.

I've had success partnering with letterboxers to carve for me in the
past, including, Scarab, Choi, Team Randalstik, Talking Turtle, and
Jacks, and I seek to add to my list of carving partners to place some
NY boxes. If you like to carve but don't like to plant, or if you
enjoy carving enough to carve a stamp or two for another's letterbox,
send me an e-mail at seh-letterbox@.... I'll post my ideas
here in another post later today or tomorrow.

You will get full credit for the stamp image. The catch this time is
that if you are willing to carve, I'd need to get the stamp from you
before I head home on December 23rd.

Many thanks in advance!!
