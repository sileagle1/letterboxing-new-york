Jiggs Clan - Home and Happy (Almost!)

From: Pete and Erin | Date: 2005-08-30 04:08:31 UTC
Hi all!

The two Jigglets and I want to take this opportunity to pass on our
thanks to Daina and Kevin in particular, and to all those who helped
organize, donate, cook, box, attend etc etc etc!

It was an absolutely fabulous weekend, where, through the Sprite's
good graces, I was able to box my face off, along with Loch Wench,
Catbead and Dagonell, on Saturday, and then return Sunday with
Craftymouse and Dandelion to againbeat up the Park in Monroe County!

We actually ran into local Rochester Boxers (Haiku Dada, Grasshopper
and Panda) on Sunday, and Ladybug (in Oswego) on Monday, that
weren't aware there was a gathering on Saturday in their own
backyard, no less! Anyhow, we met some more new faces, and were able
to do a little boxing on the side!

By our count, we managed to find 59 of the 62 boxes planted in the
park - the only ones to elude us were Puzzled #2 (even went back Sun
Afternoon for another look), Monopoly #2 (there was a film crew
there ALL Day!) and Monopoly #3 (this one may have been dragged off
by a ferocious 4 (or perhaps 2)legged animal! We even managed to
pick up the Onions' Park in Monroe County series!

Including the 10 boxes we picked up Friday, and the 3 we managed
today (yes there IS a Henry!!) we totalled 72 finds, 1 plant
(Erica's one-day 2 Hairs on the Head box), 1 HH, 27 exchanges 4
attempts, and 3 pairs of tired feet!

Now for the ALMOST part - our little Toyota's engine died about 1
mile west of Kingston, ON on Hwy 401 (like I81 or 90). Through the
good graces os of some local folks, we managed to get the car to a
garage, and then these folks were kind enough to drive us to where
my wife was able to pick us up!

Hopefully, the old girl is repairable...

Sooo, we now sit at P33 F497 X122, tired , and (mostly) happy!

Thanks again, and we'll keep you posted!

Pete aka Jiggs
