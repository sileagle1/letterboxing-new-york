I just planted a box here in SF that's of wacky and fun. I'm sharing
the opening paragraphs of the clues with LbCNY, as I'm sure you all
need to seek help. The box is called "The Therapeutist". Here goes:

You're a letterboxer. You sneak around, avoiding the public's
attention. You don't use your real name. You have a fetish for
Rubbermaid products. You play with pink stuff and sharp objects. One
minute you're Dirty Harry, the next minute you're Nancy Drew. You
exhibit obsessive-compulsive behavior, and your head throbs from
pondering cryptic instructions.

You need help. Seek the Therapeutist.

I'm not so sure, though, that Magritte, the surrealist
painter's "Therapeutist" will cure you. Face it. You're beyond help.
But maybe this surreal letterbox will make reality seem closer to
the reality that non-letterboxers experience.

No appointment is necessary. Drop-ins are welcome.

So if you come to San Francisco, be sure to wear some flowers in
your hair, and see the Therapeutist.
