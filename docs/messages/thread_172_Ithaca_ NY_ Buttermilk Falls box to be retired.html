Downstream Deliverance, one of two boxes at Buttermilk Falls in Ithaca, NY,
will be retired not long after the Finger Lakes, NY gathering this coming
Saturday. This adopted box has a full logbook and a cracked container. The
out-of-state planter didn't care if the box remained in place or not when I
contacted her to adopt it a couple of years ago, so that combined with the
new NY state park geocache permit policy (which may or may not apply to
letterboxes) has led to my decision to retire the box instead of sprucing
it up.

If you'll be in town for the gathering, don't miss it! I highly recommend
parking at the lower lot so that you can fully appreciate the beauty of the
gorge as you hike (parking details are in the clues). Buttermilk Falls is
truly spectacular, so take advantage of this last opportunity to find one
of Central NY's oldest boxes while you still can.

TurtleMcQ
