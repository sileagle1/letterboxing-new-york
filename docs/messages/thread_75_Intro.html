Intro

From: kcdaniels | Date: 2005-01-25 06:34:08 UTC
I've been lurking on this list for quite a while, so I thought I'd introduce
myself. My name is Kaycee and I live outside of Buffalo. I had the
pleasure of meeting several of you at the WNY event last November. I
started LBing last spring and, though I don't get the Frequent Boxer Award,
I do enjoy getting out there when I can. The WNY event was actually a big
shot in the arm for us. Previous to that, we had gone out a couple times
and had been unable to find the boxes and we were losing steam. Finding so
many boxes in one day was GREAT, and we went back to Royalton Ravine a few
days later to get some more!

I drag two kids along with me, but haven't been too successful at getting
the hubby out there. I'm a home schooling SAHM, so we sometimes try to grab
a box driving home from music lessons, gym, etc. I love introducing other
families to this hobby, and I've taken a few along with me when I've gone
boxing.

I guess you'd have to call me a carve-o-phobic. I've never been the
artistic sort, and the unhappy truth is that the 8 yr. old is the best
artist in the house. However, after winning the carving kit at the WNY
event (thank you, catbead!!!), I felt a certain moral responsibility to give
it my best shot. I have 6 stamps ready and will do some planting as soon as
it is possible to do so without taking a snow blower along...

Happy Boxing,
Kaycee
