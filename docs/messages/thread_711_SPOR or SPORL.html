From: JuneMcAllister | Date: 2008-01-05 21:59:27 UTC
Actually there is a pretty thorough glossary on Silent Doug's site at http://www.letterboxing.info/   .............Though SPOR isn't on it unless I missed it.  Actually SPOR isn't an "official" term, if any of the terms could be called official. People just make a lot of them up as they go along and other people start using them and they become a part of the language. Some people just tend to forget that not everyone knows them, just like they tend to forget that not everyone knows what they are talking about when they regale everyone with their boxing adventures, fun though they may be. Some things become kind of an inside thing, though I don't think it's done intentionally to make anyone feel left out. But that can happen anyway, intentional or not.
 
Have you joined the new boxers group on yahoo? That's a good place to ask questions. It was created because new people would be asking the same questions over and over on the other lists. That can get a little irritating. But the new list is for asking all of them.
 
Welcome to boxing and remember to always **rehide well**!!. :) It might also be wise to avoid the flames and arguements that get started on the usa list and on Atlas Quest. That can be discouraging.
