From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-12-16 18:32:33 UTC
May I suggest a method? Bring binoculars or a camera and hang it from
your neck. Also bring an extra coat or blanket and throw it over the
grill. Lay your journal on top along with a pen. Pretend to be
watching/photographing birds and pretend to "scribble" in your
journal. While writing with one hand, slip your other hand under the
coat/blanket-covered grill and search for the box. When found..hang
your binoculars or camera from your neck and stamp in. Trust me...few
people will bother watching you. Even if you are only 6 foot away.

Move to another grill and do the same until you find both boxes. Works
like a charm. :)
