Re: merging groups?

From: Daina | Date: 2005-08-18 19:56:46 UTC
I thought about this, too and came to the conclusion that really, all
we're doing is merging in name. All the other aspects of New York
letterboxing have already been merged (well already go to each
other's regions and box/gather), so all that really was left was to
merge boards. I figured since I put the idea out there, I probably
was the one who needed to make the name switch. On a tech note, I
figured rather than make a bunch of people rejoin a whole new group,
I'd just change LBWNY and let the few who aren't already signed up
click and join.

Again, I hope no one feels like they HAVE to join the LBNY group. It
looked like a lot of you wanted to have just one board to deal with,
so I made one. For people that don't, no change is necessary. If it
turns out this doesn't work the way I'd hoped, then I can just
reinstate LBWNY in a couple of keystrokes (good thing it's summer!) I
know I'm still a part of LBNE, LBGLK and LBMA just so I can see
what's going on within a day's drive, so if people choose keep an
LBCNY membership and the LBNY one, it won't affect anything.
