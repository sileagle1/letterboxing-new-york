Hey there ... I don't live in Central NY (live in Boston now), but I
thought I'd stop in and say hello anyway! I grew up in the Finger
Lakes (just north of Canandaigua) and have a ton of great ideas for
boxes back there, but haven't been able to put any together yet.

I was a little worried that the more 'boxers from central and western
NY came along, the more likely I was to lose the spots I'm thinking
of to one of you, but that's OK. I look at it this way now -- as I
won't be able to care for the boxes, I've got a lot of potential
babysitters!

Anyway, best of luck with the group!


CPAScott

From: catbead1 | Date: 2004-10-24 02:26:44 UTC
We were in Boston last weekend and had time for only one box, The
Battle of Bunker Hill. I've had my eye on this one for a year and we
finally had the opportunity to go after it. We even replaced the
logbook (which was a tight fit). One thing though, when we found it,
it was out in the open next to an empty chip bag and ripe for the
trash collectors. We rehid it so that a boxer looking for it would
find it, but with luck no one else will.

Give it another try, and when you do find it, give the fish's nose a
rub for luck.

Cheers,
catbead
