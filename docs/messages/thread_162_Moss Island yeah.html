For those of you looking for a fun day trip, I suggest you check out
Moss Island in Little Falls, NY. A very fun place to explore with all
sorts of cool rock formations and "potholes". I grew up not far from
here so on a visit home I planted a letterbox but I intend to plant
more.

Anyone else have any cool ideas for an unusual daytrip?
