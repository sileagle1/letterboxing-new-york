Ghost of Seneca Hill strikes again!

From: thespiritofmrpigeon | Date: 2005-06-15 13:24:22 UTC
Well, I'm pretty sure that the Ghost is trying to tell us something!
A storm must have struck the Riverside Cemetery in the past few
days. We received word that the giant tree to the left of the tree
that's hosting the letterbox had fallen over right on top of the
tree with box. We checked it out last night and decided to remove
the box until clean-up is complete in the area. It shouldn't be too
long, although that was one MASSIVE tree that fell, and it's
currently resting on top of the other tree. It's also in danger of
falling and destroying several very large and important headstones.

We think the Ghost is letting us know that SHE KNOWS about her
letterbox. How interesting that the only tree in the entire cemetery
to take a fall during a storm should happen to land right where we
planted a letterbox!

The creepiness factor for this box just went up!

We'll post again when we've replanted. There may be a little
something extra in regards to this box at that time.

Here's a shout-out to WINK for letting us know about our Ghost:
Duuude! You're our hero!!!! Long live the Wizards of Oz!
