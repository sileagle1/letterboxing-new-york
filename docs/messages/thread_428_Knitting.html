From: knitica | Date: 2005-12-20 18:57:26 UTC
I have only made one hooked rug which I hooked with handspun
wool/mohair singles, because that's the sort of thing I can create a
lot of. I got the other supplies from a vendor at the Finger Lakes
Fiber Festival, which usually sports 2 or 3 rug hooking vendors, who
have chunks of really beautiful fabric, some of it hand dyed, for the
sort of hooking you're talking about. An online search for rug
hooking should turn up some possibilities.

Don't remember where you're located, but I remember seeing rug
hooking stuff at the needlework shop in Canandaigua, too.
