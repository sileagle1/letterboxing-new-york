For you knitters, spinners and weavers

From: ROC'nRON | Date: 2005-12-18 21:30:54 UTC
I have a friend in Rochester who lived and worked for five weeks the summer before last at Steam Valley Fiber Farm in PA. In addition to feeding and cleaning up after the sheep and goats, she learned to card, dye and spin wool. She attended some shows and won some prizes for her work. Her cost to stay at the farm was minimal. She now owns a couple of looms and enjoys weaving. She loves what she's doing and that has resulted in her turning out some truly beautiful work. I thought the knitters among you might find that of interest.   ~R'nR
