It has come to my attention that not everyone from LBCNY is aware of
this Saturday's party. Let me fill you in....

Paul in SF is the founder of this group but moved to San Francisco.
He still has family in the Utica-Rome area (as well as letterboxes!)
so he periodically comes home to visit. He has graciously offered to
host a party this Sat at 1pm (along with his neices and nephews) in
Oriskany, NY. All LBCNY boxers are welcome to come and swim, carve,
talk, exchange and box! He and his family are providing burgers,
hotdogs and soda...and the rest of us can bring a sidedish or dessert
to share.

Paul really needs a headcount..so please RSVP to him directly at
pgonyea@... so he can send you directions. Looking
forward to seeing all LBCNY boxers and their families! His original
post is included below...
