From: Elba Onions | Date: 2007-04-19 16:22:24 UTC
Hi Songchick,

To drive to and from Canada a US citizen needs proof of id (driver's
license & birth certificate) this year (2007). To *fly* from Canada
to US a US citizen needs a passport. We'll need a passport to drive
starting in January 2008.

We have 4 Canadian feises this year (one next month) and the
passport wait is 12 weeks right now. And, they take your birth
certificate with the application so you're without your b.c. for the
12 weeks unless you go to the county you were born in and get an
official duplicate............. so plan on procuring a passport
between now and 2008, but plan the processing to occur during a
three month period when you will not have to cross the border.

No problem!

Are you going to CNY? What new baby?

Judith
