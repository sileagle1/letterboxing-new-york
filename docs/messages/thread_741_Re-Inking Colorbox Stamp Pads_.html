From: Tamsyn | Date: 2009-08-19 19:52:19 UTC
Hi HikerGuyEd!

Yes, I believe they can be re-inked. I have 7 different Color Box
re-inkers.
There is a full range of colors you can order, but you'll need to figure
out the
names of the colors on your petal point if possible so you don't risk
ordering
the wrong shade. Check out this Monkey House page:
http://stores.shop.ebay.com/Monkey-House-Hobby-and-Home__W0QQ_sidZ1241044?_nkw=color+box&submit=Search

Maybe they can help you figure out the color, since they sell the Petal
Points too.

Good luck!

Tamsyn
