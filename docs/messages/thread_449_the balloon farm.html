The Christmas Story

From: Cyndie | Date: 2006-01-22 18:11:41 UTC
OK..it only took me three separate trips but I finally got all of the
Christmas Story stamps by budgie. I knew the location well, as I have
my own Woodchuck Hill stamp there...and found the first one with no
problem. But 2 was so confusing! There are many a couple of cedar
groves, several stone walls, many trail signs and even several moss-
covered wedge shaped stones! I DID find a geocache (I see you did
too, SpudHund! and even got a travel bug! I had no swag with me so
left $2 in the cache instead. I am a geocacher as well but I prefer
letterboxing.) The weather turned bad, so I left for the day. I got
my sister to go back out with me yesterday when the sun came out. Of
course, half way down the trail the sun disappeared and the wind
started howling...and then the rain came. HARD. I was nearly soaked
but still managed to eliminate some potential box locations.

Today I woke up very early to a brilliant blue sky and no wind. I
went back and this time came in from the opposite direction. Ah, just
as I thought. More stone walls, wedge shaped rocks, etc. Finally
managed to find "slippery rock" and that did it for me. A very good
hide. So I am happy to say I have all 3 stamps from that series now.
Thanks for the adventure!
