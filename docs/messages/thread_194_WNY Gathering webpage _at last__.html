WNY Gathering webpage (at last!)

From: Daina | Date: 2005-06-21 02:24:36 UTC
I finally got the webpage for the gathering started...here's the link:

http://www.geocities.com/dasprite222/gathering2.htm

It you haven't signed up yet, please do! Durand Eastman Park is only an
hour and a half from both Syracuse and Buffalo, so it can't be too much
worse for anyone else in the Central NY area. Being so near a large
city makes the hotels and weekend entertainment easier to come by.
Those of you with little ones can promise them a trip to Seabreeze, the
amusement park that's about 5 minutes from the park. And, of course,
Lake Ontario is all over the place!

If you can't come, box donations would be GREATLY appreciated! We need
someone with a a good carving tool and a steady eye to carve up some
stamps for the area to be placed out and about (heck, even a bad
carving tool and a blind eye...we ain't picky, we just like boxing!!!)
We could also use some raffle items, like stamp pads, nifty logbooks,
ink or ink markers, etc.

Contact us offlist if you'd like to help out...hope to see you (or your
boxes) there!

Re: WNY Gathering webpage (at last!)

From: Daina | Date: 2005-06-22 00:40:09 UTC
There are no dumb questions, only dumb answers...I'm good at the latter!

Seriously, we make gathering as painless as possible. You send us the
box, we plant it for you. When you come, bring food that ISN'T hotdogs
to share (we have drinks, but if you have special stuff you like, bring
that too!). We'll give you a booklet of clues for the boxes in the
park, you look up the ones that aren't in the park. The idea is to get
to box as much as possible, then sit around and talk about boxing as
much as possible. Then we raffle things off, out of towners go out to
dinner with us, then we go home (or to hotels) and box all day Sunday!

Pretty simple, really! Have you signed up at AQ yet???

The S & H
