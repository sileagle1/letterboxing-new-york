AtlasQuest

From: paulonthepark | Date: 2004-10-03 18:48:15 UTC
I've added a link (in the Links section) to a fairly new site,
called, AtlasQuest. It's a letterboxing site, but with some new and
useful features. You can set up a logbook to track your plants and
finds, search for letterboxes based on a location you select, search
for other letterboxers,track hitchhikers, and much more. It also
distuinguishes between the original planters of a box and the kind
folks who have adopted them. International boxes are included,too.
Plus, there are more features coming in the future...

Please take a look and register to use this helpful site.

http://atlasquest.com/

Paul in SF

We love it, too! It works great with LBNA, since you can just link
the clues to your LBNA clue page, or your own home page. It also lets
you keep track of all your P's and F's, including virtual, HHs and
travelers. Also has a hall of fame for 25 and 50 P's & F's, but if
the box you found hasn't been logged by the creator yet, you can't
post it as a find.

Everyone sign up quick! :-)
