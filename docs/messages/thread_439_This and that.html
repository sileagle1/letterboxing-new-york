From: Cyndie | Date: 2006-01-07 15:42:01 UTC
It's nice to see some new posting going on here. We New Yorkers
aren't TOTALLY stopped by snow, are we? I am happy to report that
I've seen some new plants in the Syracuse area (Try Budgie's I.M.
Pei's Box, for example) and there has been an date change for the
Finger Lakes Gathering in June. I highly recommend this gathering!

The plans for Desperately Seeking Sun are chugging right along.
Donated boxes are trickling in, as well as some cool raffle prizes.
Let us be your re-gifting headquarters! Absolutely hate that scarf
Aunt Millie knit for you? Send it in! End up with more #1 blades than
you can use? We'll take 'em! Think someone else would enjoy a pair of
your favorite hiking socks? We agree! Contact me by email
(lockwench@...) and I'll let you know how to get them to us.

There are all sorts of unique boxes for this event, with some carved
by premiere boxers from all over the country, so please try to join
us. Some are truly unique or twisted.....muah ha ha! We have
a "Chocolate Game" for the kids with a special prize and awesome
Basket Raffles including a Finder's Basket, a Planter's Basket, a
Carver's Basket, a Mini-box Basket and an I Love New York basket. For
those of you who are coming in from out of town..this time is also
Syracuse's Winterfest, with all sorts of cool events if you can stay
for the entire weekend. Did I mention that there will be people
crawling all over local parks looking for the special Winterfest
Medallion (worth $1,000)? We now have a perfect cover
story....."We're looking for the Medallion!"

And don't forget our Desperately Seeking Suds event. This event is
not only for those who attend Sun, but for anyone who wants to meet
up with us at Kelley's Restaurant and Irish Pub. This will be the
Mecca of Traveling Event stamps, as well as a chance to pick up those
personal stamps you might have missed. There will be an event stamp
as well as a permanent stamp hidden at Kelley's called "Last Call."
We have our own room and you don't have to eat or drink to come join
us. Just show up! Although anyone who is interested in having dinner
should rsvp on Atlasquest or send me a note so that we have an
approximate head count.

The official countdown starts February 1st. I hope we'll pull some of
you out of your winter hibernation.
