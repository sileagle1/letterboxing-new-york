Do you remember the Choose Your Own Ending Books?? Where you read
part of the story and then choose which direction to take.

I have started planning and writing a Choose Your Own
Letterboxing Series to be planted in Northwest Indiana. I am hoping
to maintain this series over a two year period. Since each box will
be extremely critical I have a feeling it will need constant
checking on. We are already planning on carving all of the stamps
(around 25) right now in duplicate.

I am hoping there is someone here that would be willing to
proofread the story for me. If they had any kind of a D&D background
that would be extremely helpful since I would like to stay as close
to that "reality" as possible. It's been a very long time since I've
played seriously so my brain is a little fuzzy on some things.

Does this sound like a fun series to you? I am considering only
posting the availabilty of the clues to those on the Great Lakes
Board (of course everyone here now knows so if it gets done and
you're in the area lol). I already have a Michigan Family that is
willing to test the series for me once I have it done but would
prefer they not know the story ahead of time.

Thanks in advance and if anyone has any suggestions or ideas of
any other problems I might run in too I'd love to hear them.
