From: Paul Gonyea | Date: 2005-11-18 23:57:15 UTC
Buggylou,
 
Glad you made it to the Left Coast. It's very different from the East, isn't it?
 
But, no! Western Blue Jays are just extra friendly and curious -- not loud and obnoxious like the ones you're used to. He just wanted to be friends...
 
Now that you've done LA, you need to visit the Other Half of California in the North: San Francisco.
 
You can buy a loaf of bread here at a store without getting on a freeway and sitting in traffic, you don't need an agent calling you on your cell phone, no pressure to be 'fabulous' in a Hollywood style, and the city is far more aesthetically beautiful and breathtaking.
 
In fact, you can be whoever you want to be and do and wear whatever you want here in the Bay Area. People won't judge you. Your zodiac sign is more important to others than how much money you make or the car you drive.
 
It can be quirky, and eccentric, and diverse, and at the same time, you meet really fascinating people with great life stories. As for clothing outfits and hair styles -- every day has a bit of Halloween in it.
 
Well, yes,  there is the fog that makes July chilly -- but then, I don't have screens on my windows, because we have very, very few mosquitoes. I get about 3 bites a year until I fly to Utica in July. They hover in huge masses at the airport, darkening the sky, just waiting to dive for my veins!
 
What do you think, Lisascenic, across the Bay, over there in Oakland and Berkeley? What's your choice? LA or SF?
 
Paul in SF and not LA
