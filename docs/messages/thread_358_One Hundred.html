Re: [LbNY] What would you recommend?

From: Walter Quinn III | Date: 2005-10-03 17:50:18 UTC
Yep, AtlasQuest is the one you're thinking of, most likely. www.atlasquest.com.
Although, you'll want to use both sites for boxes, since not everone uses LbNA AND Atlas Quest...some use both, some use one or the other exclusively.
 
Personally, I like Atlas Quest best. I like all the features, and the virtual logbook is fun and simple to use. Plus, it lets you easily tell people how their boxes are doing when you log your finds.
 
Man, I wish i had known about letterboxing back when I was a boy scout...that would have totally rocked.
 
-Celtic Quinn
