The shelter that I would like every one to meet at is Lancaster Place.
Enter from Como park Blvd for the easiest access. Go down Quincy Ave
and stay on that road to reach Lancaster Place witch is by the tens
Cortes. Lancaster Place is enclosed and heated. It also has a kitchen
and inside bathroom. There are two grills outside as well.
http://www.erie.gov/parks/graphics/como_lancaster2.jpg
This is the park map: http://www.erie.gov/parks/como_map.asp

There are several boxes around the park such a 'Alvin and the
chipmunks', 'Hockey Series' and 'Some Familiar Friends' Just to name a
few. There will also be boxes planted that are themed on the event
which is Halloween/ fall.

I was thinking that everyone could bring there own lunch and something
to share. Then we could go to a restaurant afterward for dinner or
have dinner at Lancaster Place.
