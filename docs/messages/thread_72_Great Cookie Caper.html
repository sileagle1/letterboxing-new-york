Hi Alphabet Bandits and LbCNY,

Letterboxing finally made the newspaper here in the Watertown area!
Not an article exactly, but an Associated Press photo of someone
digging in the snow for a letterbox. It's labelled "Hunting Season"
and it reads, (and I hope I'm not doing the unforgiveable by
repeating it here even though it WAS in the paper)

Jeremy Tendler, Waterbury, Conn., digs for a Great Cookie Caper box
on Monday at **** ****, in ****. The "letterboxing" game is a
treasure hunt where participants try to find boxes based on clues.

Sorry for all the asterisks! Before I posted this I checked the
clues (phew, good thing!)and found it's a mystery box series so I
don't want to be the spoiler!

I don't think this article is going to create any big letterboxing
rush here in the North Country!

MTnestRobin
