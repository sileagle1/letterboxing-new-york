Summer Reports and Fall Plans-

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-09-01 12:57:19 UTC
Whew...my summer box check up has been completed. I have 7 boxes to
replace and two more boxes that need some love and attention. I have
to thank Catbead for all her great planting instruction. There are
STILL some Catbead boxes that I have yet to find, but I won't give up!

I am most distraught about the loss of the I LOVE NY Hitchhiker
Hostel. I still think the crossroads area is the best and most
convenient location for travelers but I am testing a new hiding place
before I plant another Hostel. I have a "dummy" hostel in place
(hidden a couple of weeks ago) and I will be checking it next week to
see if the hiding spot works. If it has remained untouched, I will be
planting the newly carved HHH stamp and a new container. I will test
it with my own HH's first and if it all works, I will call for new
additions. I'd like to get it up and running before the wealth of New
England/Mid- Atlantic gathers begin.

Still waiting to hear back from a friends in the state park system and
then I will publish my findings. The only park I did not directly ask
about letterboxes was at Clark Reservation. There are too many boxes
there from various letterboxers for me to even THINK about speaking
for all. I don't want to jeopardize anyone else's boxes.
Now that summer is about over, I look forward to FINDING boxes. I
plant and maintain in the summer, but fall is my favorite season to
hunt for boxes. So maybe I will see some of you out on the trails!
