Letterboxing New York Logo?

From: Cyndie | Date: 2006-02-04 17:54:12 UTC
I was wondering if anyone would be interested in designing a logo for
our group. The Pacific Northwest LB group is currently trying to
decide on a patch logo and I think it would be cool if we had our
own. You can see the many designs they are considering by going to:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/LbPNW/?yguid=224892657

You might have to join in order to see them in the photos section,
but I am sure that's cool with them.

The reason I am bringing this up...if we can come up with a logo than
we can have some cool stuff. I recently attended my Alpiners Hiking
group meeting and one of our members put our all black logo on the
corner of a red bandana...and we were able to get really nice
bandanas for $4 a piece, and dog bandanas ( those triangular ones)
for only $5.

Of course, I love the idea of a patch too. Maybe a patch AND a
bandana? I had considered getting some bandanas for sale )at cost)
for Desperately Seeking Sun with the DSS stamp image, but then
decided it would be better to have these for a larger group, such as
LBNY. What do you think?

Lock Wench
