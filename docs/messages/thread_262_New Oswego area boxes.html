Good News for CNY Boxes!

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2005-08-08 21:07:07 UTC

Well, at least I think it's good news.  If you got the Sunday paper this week ( Aug. 7)...check the STARS section. On page 11 ( the travel section) someone wrote in for day trip suggestions and the travel writer suggested letterboxing!  She  said: "Letterboxing, www.letterboxing.org.  is a free, fun activity, especially with children. Little boxes are hidden in places of interest all over the United States. There are many in Central New York. Go on a "treasure hunt' using the clues on the Web site."

So be prepared, CNY placers....you may be getting more activity in your box journals!

Lock Wench

Re: [LbCNY] Re: Derby Hill &dogs

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2005-08-10 04:22:44 UTC
Jen -
 
I contacted Derby Hill regarding dogs. They replied :
 
Hi Cyndie,
 
Yes, dogs on a leash are perfectly fine. 
 
Though it's a fun and beautiful spot all year 'round, I hope you'll be able to visit us during March-May when the spring migration hawk count is taking place.  On days with south winds during that period, we may have thousands of hawks pass overhead!
 
---David Wheeler.
 

Hope that clears it up!!
