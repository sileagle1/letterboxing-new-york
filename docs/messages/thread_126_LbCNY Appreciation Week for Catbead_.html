LbCNY Appreciation Week for Catbead!

From: paulonthepark | Date: 2005-04-20 02:17:42 UTC
I'd like to declare this week as an LBCNY Appreciation Week in honor
of Catbead, who has done a great job of taking over the moderating
of this Yahoo group from the distant me, Paul in SF.

I think that her Topics of the Week keep this Yahoo group actively
thinking and participating. It's a great way to allow everyone to
contribute when and if they want to. She's a great resource for
ideas and knowledge.

I've very pleased to see how this group is growing, and most
importantly, how well everyone shares and works together to bring
letterboxing to NY. This group seems to be free of competing
interests and bickering... Hooray! That's a compliment to everyone
as members, and also to Catbead as our moderator.

SO, folks! Please join me in a BIG ROUND OF APPLAUSE and a CHEER
loud enough to be heard in San Francisco for the great job Catbead
has done!

[Major applause and wild cheering heard everywhere, please!].

Happy Catbead Appreciation Week!

Paul in SF
