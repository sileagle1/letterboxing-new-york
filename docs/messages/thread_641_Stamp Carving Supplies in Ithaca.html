At 12:06 PM 12/16/2006, you wrote:

>Does anybody know anywhere in the Ithaca area that sells them??

I haven't had much luck finding them locally but I think the Cornell
bookstore stocks the pink stuff from time to time. Also, McNamara's near
the mall sometimes carries Mastercarve erasers. I've switched to PZKut,
though, so local availability doesn't matter any more. It's much more
affordable and a great carving material, plus the owner is an avid
letterboxer. She also sells carving materials besides just carving medium,
things like thick foam to back your stamps and carving blades.

http://www.stampeaz.com/

She can fit 4 sheets into one priority mail envelope, which helps reduce
the shipping portion of the cost. If you don't want 4 sheets, you might
find someone in the area willing to split an order with you.

Have fun carving, and enjoy this warm weather as you go out boxing!
