From: Bridget Lichtinger | Date: 2005-11-21 19:06:20 UTC
The past two weeks have been so much fun. One of my best friends -
Trailhoundz has gotten me and my other best friend - Artichoke - hooked
on letterboxing. The weather has been beautiful the past two weekends
for hunting. I have found 25 stamps so far, and most of them in my own
backyard (so to speak) and I NEVER knew. With winter on its way I will
be busy carving to plant letterboxes in the Spring. Woo hoo!!

I know Trailhoundz through greyhounds. I have five of my own - Alyssa,
Maggie, Sally, Rimmy and John...and we have two cats...Willie and Echo.
No kids..just animals :-)

Just wanted to say how much I enjoy letterboxing and thank you to all
the artists out there with your beautiful stamps.

Budgie

P.S. Happy Thanksgiving too!
