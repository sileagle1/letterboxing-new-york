From: shirl yaremo | Date: 2007-08-22 01:46:41 UTC
I don't know what anyone else would advise you, but this is my advice.  I live less than 20 miles from NF NY and I wouldn't go out there after dark.  It is a very depressed (job wise) and depressing place to go.  Most of the city is very dirty and full of drug dealers..  I worked there for 5 years as a drug counselor so this is first hand information.  The Canadian side is much cleaner and cheaper.  Cheaper partially due to the fact that US currency is 14cents on the $1.  If you exchange the money in a bank you will get $1.14 for each dollar of US.  If you exchange it in a store, not so much.  My advice would be to go to a bank or the casino in NF , Ontario and exchange all your money before you spend american money there.  The store may give you .10 on the $1.  If you stay in NF Ontario and plan on going LB in NY, it's a very short trip across the bridge from Canada to NY.  Just make sure you have photo ID to cross the bridge.  The park on this side is very nice, as is the aquarium, but otherwise there isn't much here.  I'm not sure what US sights you can access from the Canadian side, but some other really cool things to do here are the Maid of the Mist, a boat ride that takes you right next to the falls.  A beautiful ride.  Also, the tour underneath the falls where you can experience the actual power of the falls because you're in a cave like place underneath it.  You can still do these things by staying in Canada and crossing the bridge.  If you're close enough, you can walk across the bridge.  Go online and get as many coupons as you can for the previously mentioned adventures.  I haven't been up there to get boxes yet, but some were just replanted on this side down in the gorge.  Commonly known as Devil's Hole.  I am going to NF, NY tomorrow to pick someone up to come and work on my computer, but I spend as little time there as possible.  Hope this helps some.  I know it sounds awful knocking our own side like this, but it's all true.  When the city gets money from the government for renewal, it never happens.  Grandma51 aka Shirley
