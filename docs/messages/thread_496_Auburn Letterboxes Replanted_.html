Auburn Letterboxes Replanted!

From: Jen Z | Date: 2006-04-18 12:45:35 UTC
Hi everyone,
 
I am slowly replanting my Auburn letterboxes that I pulled for the winter (and replacing the ones that were lost/vandalized!). As of this past weekend, AUBURN DOUBLEDAYS (1 box at Casey Park) and LUCY AND CLIFFORD's WILDLIFE ADVENTURES (9 box series at Cayuga Community College) have been replanted/replaced.  New clues have been written for L & C's Wildlife Adventures... hopefully they will be harder to find so the college students don't root them out and trash them like last time, LOL.
 
I am working on bookbinding some logbooks for additions to the Historic Auburn series -currently it is 3 boxes but I have upped it to 6. :o)  If the weather is good, the pups and I will be planting them this weekend.
 
I'm so excited spring is here!!! :o)
 
~Trailhoundz

LOL, they do have a lot of dead stuffed animals in there... perhaps if it is small you could put one in some bear cub's mouth! I'm amazed at how they (these caches) can be put so -urban, for lack of a better word- and still not be discovered. That is pretty crafty if I do say so myself! Very cool.
 
Thanks for the kind words about my boxes. *blush*. Hopefully I will be a better planter this year so they aren't stumbled upon by non-boxers and stolen/vandalized/trashed. But I AM more experienced this time around, so hope springs eternal!
