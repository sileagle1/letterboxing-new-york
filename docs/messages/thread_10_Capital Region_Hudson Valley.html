Hey Central NYers! I just saw the post about this on the LbNA list
and joined. I'm located in the Capital Region, but my
partner/teammate and i like traveling to the Hudson Valley, and we'd
be willing to travel a little farther west in search of good
letterboxes.

We've been getting more into urban letterboxes, as neither of us is a
proficient enough hiker to do some of the more difficult trails (i
have really bad knees and asthma, so i find anything more than a
moderate hike very difficult).

We're planning a few boxes and possibly some microboxes in the Capital
Region. I'll post to this list when they're up. In the meantime, i'd
be pleased to make the online acquaintance of anyone from our area!

Circus
