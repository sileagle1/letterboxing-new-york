I am pleased to announce that RiverOtter is our 100th member. No
prize, just bragging rights! I am even more pleased to see that
Fireball has been planting and bringing new people into the Buffalo
area and LBWNY and getting some more finders for those Buffalo boxes!
And more finders means more boxes and more boxes means more boxers and
more boxers means more gathers and...well you get the picture. :)

If you haven't been to a gather yet, you are really missing half the
fun of boxing. It's a great way to find boxers to box with, for
families with kids to find others, to put a face with a stamp and to
try some great food. And of course there are boxes to find! Be sure to
check out the August gather at Darien Lake State Park hosted by New
York's own Sprite and the Highlander. They are pros at gathers; having
hosted several, attended more, and have even written a list of tips
for attending a gather for the first time. You can find the tip list
on Atlasquest or Mark and Sue Pepe's blog. To read more about the
event and/or to sign up..check out www.atlasquest.com
