Hi Guys,

I also apologize for posting infrequently while reading everyone else's
messages, and I second the "Thank you Catbead!!!" for keeping this list
going.

We've carved a lot of our stamps out of the Mastercarve stuff. I've
never been in a store that sells PZ cut so I don't know what it is.
I've heard people mention "the pink stuff" and I don't know what that
is, either.

My favorite thing to do is carve on fun erasers. I bought a ton of them
at Office Max after the "Back to School" hoopla was over. I have round,
smiley ones that I like to carve, and also they had ones that look like
coins. They make the best hitchhikers. I really enjoy finding a stamp
that someone made out of something unusual.

As far as logbooks go, that's an area I need to work on. I can sit and
carve anywhere, but my craft area at home is just a mess, so it's a real
challenge to move stuff, find what I need, clear a space, and be
inspired to create wonderful logbooks. I tend to run spiral notebooks
through a bandsaw and get on with my life.

Hopefully my logbook endeavors will improve....... :)
