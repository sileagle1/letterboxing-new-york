I think it's so interesting that all greyhound owners are excellent

***************

When I was in art school, the school encouraged students to have dogs
and to bring them to the studios (safety precaution for late night
walks home). Greyhounds were a favorite studio dog. After a tough
life at the track, these sweet pups liked nothing better than to nap
in front of the heaters in the life drawing studios.

I loved the other doggies, too, especially any really cuddly pit bull
terrier. One friend of mine had pit bulls that would snuggle so hard
that they would knock you over. When those dogs get enough love,
they're just wonderful!

Now, I'm off to bed with my posse of pussycats. I love the pooches,
but don't own any.
