Added my Utica Zoo letter box series!

From: strawberryshortkook | Date: 2007-08-18 19:53:11 UTC
Hi everyone!

I was planning on adding maybe three or four letter boxes up at the
Utica Zoo. Welllllll. . .I was overly ambitious and added 12! Yes,
it's true. . . my first planted letter boxes and there are twelve of
them! You can search for them on either Atlas Quest or LB.org, either
one. I am hoping families will have a BALL with these, as there are
clues to figure out which animal will be on the stamp, and all stamps
are hidden near the animals they represent at the zoo. Most of the
employees at the zoo know where the letter boxes are, and everyone
thinks it's a great idea to get more people to visit our zoo.

Thanks to everyone who helped me with ideas, carving, etc., along the
way. I will be at the zoo periodically, so hopefully I'll catch up
with some of you while you're searching for the boxes!

Mary
Strawberry Shortkook
