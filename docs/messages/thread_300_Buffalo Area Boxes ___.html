From: Daina Burke | Date: 2005-08-24 02:42:18 UTC
Well, there the Elba Onions' boxes along the thruway, of course and then there are a few right NEAR the thruway as well, depending on how far you want to go.Canal Lock 32 is close to the Rochester section of the thruway. Little Cabin at Indian Falls is maybe a mile or two off, while Flying J is right at the Pembroke exit. There's quite a few Batavia boxes that require you to go into town, but Batavia is pretty small and right on the thruway, too.
 
Faith Hope, & Patience seems to be popular with folks cruising through and of course, the Niagara Falls boxes if you head that far up. That's all I can think of offhand...an Atlas Quest search will defintely yield more.

From: BabyPink BagPiper - Elba Onions | Date: 2005-08-26 17:07:49 UTC
Hi Guys,

I'm here.

We have been checking our thruway boxes and replacing them as
needed. Almost all the logs have been replaced; they were either
full or only had one or two pages left.

Sometimes they're not there when we check on them, then they're there
a few days later. The Thruway is a busy place, especially in the
summer.

Patience, people. They're worth the wait because the logs are cool --
people just passing through who's stamps we've never seen before.

Judith
