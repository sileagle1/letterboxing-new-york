Northern NY Report!

From: jiggs11onlb | Date: 2007-05-24 03:26:45 UTC
Hello all!

Was finally able to take a "boxing " day and do some serious boxing
in NY. Original plan was to make another trip to Oswego, but
becvause of the uncertain status of a bunch of the boxes, esp at
Rice Creek Field Station...I decided to travel through Watertown,
Lowville, Boonville, Parish, Central Square and Brewerton, then
tryiong for a box or two in Oswego. Here's the lowdown...

I picked up 16 of a possible 19 boxes, plus a HH

Here's the rundown...

Thousand Islands Park...gone. Found the spot ok.. but looks to be a
teen/fisherman hangout area, judging by the broken beer bottles and
butts.

Orpaned Elephant (Watertown)- great spot, lots of visitors, esp
younger ones!

Was going to try for for Rushing Water near Lowville, but
unfortunately, the clue doesnt say where to pick up the road.

Fountain (all Boonville)
Liberty and Justice
Over The Canal
Remember.. All great carvings in very kid accessible areas

Family Tree (3) Nice series, nice spot, well-carved. Like these
kinds of boxes that have real meaning to someone.

Salmon (Orwell) very pretty spot, and great waterfall!

Trick or Treat (3) (Parish) another neat cemetery - had to practice
my stealth as the groundskeeper was trying to cut the grass! Handy
camera to the rescue as I did my best taphophile imitation!

Bird of a Different Color #4 - (Toad Harbor) found this little guy
safe and sound.. and enjoying the company of the Slitherin Snake HH!
Now have 4 of the 7 in this series.

Flaky boxes (Brewerton) picked up 3 more that we missed in Feb...

Bentley Snowflake
A Simple Snowflake
An Alaskan Snowflake all well worth the stop!

Wanted to pick up the other 6 we missed, but couldnt figure out
where the Yellow trail was...!

Tried to find
Steve Irwin.. vegetation made it pretty difficult to see the forest
floor, so had to leave him Crikey!

Finally, off to Oswego..

tried for Ladybug's Fuzzy Caterpiller - great walk, but alas, it
appears he has metamorphed and flown away!

Daylight became my enemy at this point, so had to leave the other 25
or so Oswego boxes for another trip!

Thanks to all you wonderful planters!

Jiggs
