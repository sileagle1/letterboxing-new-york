Introducing myself...

From: Lisa Lazar | Date: 2005-05-10 18:55:50 UTC
Hello all

Just wanted to say hello on this forum. I'm a new-ish 'boxer. I
live in the San Francisco Bay Area nine months of the year, and
spend the summers in Cooperstown, where I work for the Glimmerglass
Opera.

Be prepared! I plan to plant as many boxes as I can this summer.
And I expect you all to come find 'em! I've got five stamps carved
and ready to plant.

So, here's a question. In the Bay Area, nobody includes ink pads
with their boxes. (I've only seen one, and it was in a hitch
hiker.) It seems the consensus is that this would fall into the
accident waiting to happen category. The 'boxers I know all carry
ink and pens with them. Is this standard procedure in NY? I worry
about ink making it through the winter, particularly as I won't be
able to check up on my boxes for months at a time.

I'm hoping to meet some of you in Ithaca later this summer.

Cheers
Lisascenic
