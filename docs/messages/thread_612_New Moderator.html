From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-10-17 05:21:50 UTC
By the way, I just wanted to let you all know that in about a week
there will be a new moderator for this board. I took this duty over
for Catbead, who had generously offered to handle the moderator duties
after our founder, Paul in SF, had moved to California. Paul is from
Oriskany and was instrumental in getting boxing off the ground in
upstate New York. He still makes an annual pilgrimage so many of you
may get a chance to meet him in the future.

Catbead took on the duty for quite a while, and then I took over for
her when she needed a break. Celtic Quinn has generously offered to
take over for ME, as I have recently begun a new job and I am also
planning to begin graduate courses at some point. His duties will
begin once he has reliable power again, as the Buffalo area was really
socked with some damage from that last storm.

I hope I have kept the list talking, for a list such as this dies when
there is no discussion. Many of you now know I am a person full of
opinions, but I enjoy hearing others' opinions as I learn so much and
have adjusted my own outlook after hearing of your experiences. So
thank you for taking the time to post and thank you for putting
yourself "out there." It's also been a great place to promote New
York boxing events, and we now have 4 annual gatherings in the
state...with many more mini-gatherings popping up. It has been my
intention to help connect all the little pockets of New York boxing,
and make this board a safe and respectful place to discuss boxing
issues while still maintaining and nurturing close friendships.

I will continue to post from time to time, but it may not be the full
length novels you have come to expect from me...lol. Thanks again,
everyone...and let's help Celtic Quinn in his new position as
moderator! I am sure he will develop a style and demeanor all his own.

LW
