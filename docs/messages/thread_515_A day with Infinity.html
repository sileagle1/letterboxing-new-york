A day with Infinity

From: wink2267 | Date: 2006-05-12 02:02:49 UTC
Well, I must tell y'all about a cool day letterboxing......

I got up this morning and ran some errands with my wife. I asked
her if she wanted to play "hooky" and go letterboxing, but she
couldn't. I, on the other hand, did. I headed up to capture
Yankeechiks's new boxes. Soo.......... I get to one of the new
ones, and what is sitting on the location of the box?!!???!!
Several construction workers, ....and folks....they weren't moving.
AT ALL! So I waited...and waited...and....yup....waited. They
said "hello" and smiled back. OH!.....and I waited. I pretended to
watch the crane lift crap. I made fake phone calls. I then, after
2+ hours....gave up. I had driven an hour to claim nothing. I
called Craftymouse with my frustrations and story. I needed to save
the box, and would have to come back again. *shrug* Oh well, it
was a cool place, and I'd bring the camera that I forgot in the car
next time. Well in my conversation with CM, she noted that Infinity
had mentioned that she was going to be headed up this way. I didn't
want the same thing happening to her, as me. I called her. We
decided to meet at the Salmon Falls box for an "exchange".

I pulled into the lot, and met the F-man. The gentleman used the F
word a BUNCH. EVEN MORE THAN I DO!!! He seemed to use it as a
noun, adjective, verb....all kinds of cool ways. I quickly grabbed
the camera and headed down the trail. DING! There she was. we
introduced ourselves and proceeded to box away. After
getting "Naked Trees" done, we decided to "capture" the FF certs for
all the 3 new boxes that Yankeechik put out. Yes, we (I) went back
north to the construction site after dropping my car off in
Pulaski. She drives as fast as me :) It was nice to see other
80+'ers out there. ANYHOW.... WE GOT IT!! Nice stamp, no workers,
and got some cool photos. If I can ever figure this new camera out,
I'll post'em.

Along the way Infinity obtained "coming" and "Deep in the heart
of....". We shared some funny stories, and had an outstanding day.
Thanks to all of you ladies for helping make it possible.
Craftymouse for the info (call), Infinity for hang'n with a short
fat guy for 5 hours (not an easy task, ask 'Mouse), and Yankeechik
for the box!! Thanks ladies :) :) :)

It was a great day.
