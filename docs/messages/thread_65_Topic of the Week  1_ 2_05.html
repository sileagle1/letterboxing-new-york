So far my camouflage efforts run to wrapping regular size boxes with
brown burlap. I've often thought of gluing a piece of burlap, maybe
12" x 18" ameba shape, to the lid of a box and decorating it with the
kind of debris found in the place where it's going to be planted.
Stuff like dirt/sand/sticks/moss/etc. Doing such a thorough job that
it would completely blend in. Someday....
