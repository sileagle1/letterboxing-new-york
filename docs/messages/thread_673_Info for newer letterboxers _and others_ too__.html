Info for newer letterboxers (and others, too!)

From: Paul | Date: 2007-07-28 15:30:55 UTC
I'd like to draw LbNY members' attentions to the links listed here on
LbNY (see box at left of screen). There are many links with a lot of
helpful information.

Of the links, I especially find AtlasQuest.com to be THE most useful
site for letterboxers.

If you haven't signed up already, I highly recommended that you
consider it because Atlasquest has lots of great features: a online
logbook that shows your plants & finds, and an advanced search
function to identify letterboxes located along a trip route as well
as in a town or area. After searching you can select boxes with
checkboxes, and get all of them printed continously. That saves a lot
of paper! There are tutorials on letterboxing topics such as carving.
profiles of letterboxers, supplies that can be ordered, and of
course, mail messages.

It keeps your counts for Plants, Finds, Exchanges, and more; notifies
you when someone has recorded a Find or an Attempt on a box you
planted; and uses a complete global place name listing so that boxes
in international and obscure places can be accomodated! And there's
still more stuff that I haven't included here!!!

LbNY members, if you haven't used AtlasQuest, check it out!

Paul in SF
