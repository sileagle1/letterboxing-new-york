An Intro

From: ducacj | Date: 2004-08-23 16:02:58 UTC
Just wanted to jump on here and give a quick intro. We started
letterboxing in May of this year and have had such fun looking for
boxes. I think we've attempted and not found almost as many boxes as
we have found. We did the Oriskany boxes and the drillmaster box on
our last visit to the area. We didn't see the nettles box but are
hoping to return for a visit in Nov. so if it's possible we'll hunt
then. I'm originally from Rome and now live in Indiana where my
husband is from. We try to return to CNY a few times a year so my
parents can see the kids. We took my parents with us to find the 3rd
oriskany box and the drillmaster box and now they are interested in
helping us come up with clues and maintain boxes. So hopefully we'll
have some ready by Nov. or at the latest the spring.

The Duca Family,
Cindi, Jeff, Nick and Claire
