It's been a busy couple of weeks letterboxing wise, as I have been
busy doing search and rescue for all the boxes damaged/lost in the
flood areas. I confirmed that "Salisbury Covered Bridge" is long
gone, as is "Empire Hotel". Rescued are "Planes, Trains, Automobiles
and Boats?" which was found and repaired by Rattlesnake. Thanks,
Grant! I have retired it and will probably move it in the near
future as that area became QUITE flooded and will so again.

Checked on several boxes and found all in fine shape and marked them
as such. "Furnace Brook Trail" sustained some nasty flooding but the

little trickle of waterfall is now a full fledged waterfall! I
changed the clues slightly to reflect the recent flooding.

Pogey Pond has been slightly moved ..please check the new
clues. "The Haunting", my special glow in the dark box, is back in
place after new ink was added.

I checked on box #1 of I Never Strike the Same Place Twice, and
despite recent reports, it is safely in place. I added a few details
to the clues to make it easier to find it's hidey hole.

Tomorrow I will check on a few more....only about 20 left to check!
Thanks to everyone who sent comments regarding box area conditions
