Wonders of the World Gathering

From: tamsynboxer | Date: 2006-02-02 01:34:40 UTC
Greetings, fellow NYers, bicoastal friends, and assorted others:

This is an advance notice of the first gathering in the Capital District of New York.
It will be on October 8th, which is the Sunday of Columbus Day Weekend, in
Thacher Park (near Albany). Please see the listing on AQ's Event Page for more
information as it develops.

I know it's a long way off, but I'm hoping lots of you add us to your calenders
and come visit us! We'd like to return the hospitality we've been shown by
the wonderful boxers and hosts in Western NY, Ithaca, Oswego, NYC, and
Poughkeepsie, to name a few. Soon Syracuse will be on that list.

We hope to see you here!

Your hosts: Buggylou, phynstar, Minpin Lovers, Sahalie, Daytonatip, and Tamsyn!
(And Chile Boy, expert-finder-of-boxes and children's gathering consultant.)
