A video postcard from San Francisco

From: Paul | Date: 2007-08-17 14:43:59 UTC
Hi LbNYers -

Here's a video clip on YouTube I want to share with you as if it were
a postcard.

I choreographed a short dance piece based on square dance moves, but
without a caller  and without a square! Instead, the piece is danced
by 8 square dancers on beautiful mosaic stairs that overlook the
Pacific. I combined square dance moves with pedestrian moves to fit
the mood & theme of the piece. I believe that this was the 1st time
these square dance calls were ever danced on stairs!

The music is by Terry Riley, and is titled, `G Song", as is this dance.

Watch & enjoy! And Happy Letterboxing!

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VoeK_tLQbQk
