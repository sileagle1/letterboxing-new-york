Columbus Weekend Boxing

From: jmhlittlejohn | Date: 2004-10-12 15:57:10 UTC
Hi New Yorkers,

The Elba Onions went out this past Saturday, attempted 8 and found
all 8! Hooray! We successfully found Tripoli, Ontario County Park,
3 different fingers of the Finger Lakes Mystery, Sugar Hill,
Blueberry Patch, and Mystery at the Glen. We had a great, though
exhausting time, and a day filled with different "animal" adventures:

"Tripoli" had been "marked" by a tom cat. Stinky, stinky box but the
contents were fine (You open it - I don't want to touch it...No, you
open it!).

"Sugar Hill" was being gaurded by a sleepy looking, out-in-the-
daytime raccoon, which fortunately kept his distance (Get the pepper
spray out just in case. Pepper spray? I thought *you* brought the
pepper spray. Kids, get back in the van).

And one of the fingers had been opened by a multi-toothed animal who
fortunately only likes to chew on plastic bags and tupperware and
left the good stuff alone.

We also checked on a couple of our Thruway boxes - the NYS Insect and
the NYS Beverage - and were happy to find people had stamped in and
properly replaced them several times.

Now off to place more!

BabyPink BagPiper of the ELba Onions
