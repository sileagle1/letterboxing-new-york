From: Scott and Kathy Trefz | Date: 2004-12-04 13:36:16 UTC
I stewed on this question all week and decided that I had better answer this
before the next question comes out!

And, after all this time, I can only say............they are ALL unique!!!
In their own special way ALL the Lbs are special. Some the stamp is cool
(like the ones on the Lake Ontario Dune trails) or the locations are way
cool - (like one we visited in MASS) or something special happens between
our family at a letter box (we find a treasure in the woods - a special
frog, hear or see a different bird etc.) and so ---- I will give the lame
answer that they are ALL unique!!!

Sorry that after a whole week that is the best I could do!

Kathy (Ma of the Pookah Clan)
