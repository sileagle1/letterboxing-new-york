From: catbead1 | Date: 2004-10-02 00:59:28 UTC
We leave them out.

Last winter I wanted to see how the centrifuge tubes and ink pads
fared. Both did just fine. Although one regular sized box had a lot
of water in it when we checked in the spring. The lid was on right,
so the problem was a leaky container....but everything was ok inside
because of the double bagging.

Over the holidays visitors come by, so as long as the box isn't frozen
solid into the ground, why not box thru the winter?
