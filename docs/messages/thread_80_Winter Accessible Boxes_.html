Hey CNY,

MtnMan and I XC-skied the Lake Ontario Dune Trail this weekend and
I'm happy to say that these boxes are as winter accessible as we
planned them to be. (Though I omitted one box because I have
no reason to believe it isn't accessible because of the nature of its
hiding spot)

In my opinion these boxes are a bit more strenuous to do in winter
since I'm a wussy skier! Do keep it in mind however if you are
expecting children to do it. So CNYers...if you need a Lb fix and
haven't done these pack a lunch and have at it! Feel free to email me
if you have any additional queries.

Notes: There has been construction going on (they're building a new
gatehouse) but brochures are still available at the old gatehouse.

Since the Park is closed up for winter there is no fee, no restrooms,
and no dog exclusion (Keep him leashed though! Dogs can get shot if
they're running deer!)

Also, I'm posting clues to LbNA & AQ for two near-by Rt 81 drive-by
letterboxes!
