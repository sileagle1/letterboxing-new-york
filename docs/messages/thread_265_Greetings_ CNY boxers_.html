Just wanted to say hello from a little further east...

I had the pleasure of meeting a bunch of you at the
Finger Lakes Gathering, and am still planning to get
out to Oswego soon -- sometime after the 19th,
when Chile Boy's camp ends.

I've been working my way west and central to find
all the wonderful boxes you have planted, and hope
to see more of you in the Capital District. If anyone
should wander out this way, please let me know.
I'm always game for an exchange, and maybe I can
work in a meal, too! :-) (Cooking for you, that is!)

We've got a bunch of great boxes in our area now
(as opposed to about 8 this time a year ago), and
we're working on more... Definitely worth the trip. :-)

Sorry that I joined this list so late, but better late than
never!

Looking forward to meeting more of you through
your boxes and/or in person!

Tamsyn

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2005-08-10 17:41:20 UTC
Ah yes...Foxes Falls. One of my old haunts. I am soo glad they closed off the road as it has really brought the area back to a more pristine look. When the road used to be open there was all kinds of trash, broken glass and graffiti. It's so beautiful now though. Water level is very very low this time of year but it is gorgeous in the spring. My dad still lives within a mile of this box and I am eager to plant more boxes in my hometown of Ilion. My Russell Park series was done on the trail so the stamps are pretty simple but the memories for me make up for it.  Isn't LisaScenic's stamp great? I have another 2 boxes carved for that area but I have to get there first! My dad and I are heading west of Syracuse tomorrow so look for some boxes to be planted along the way.....
 
Lock Wench
