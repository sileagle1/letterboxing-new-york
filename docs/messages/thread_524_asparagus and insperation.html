Insperation comes in strange forms, this time it was asparagus. Last
week as I was buying the first fresh asparagus of the year inspiration
struck...... New York is a great state with a rich agricultural and
rural heratage, at least upstate. It is chock full oof great spots,
sugarbushes, farmers markets. ice cream stands, mini golf courses,
Amish stores and in the fall pumpkin patches and cider mills.
Everybody has a favorite. I intend to plant boxes during this season
in a few of those places to share with the LB brotherhood. I know some
of us have done boxes like this before. I am in fact, stealing this
idea from Mark and Sue and there Sugarbush farms LB in Woodstock VT but
I challenge all NY boxers to do the some these boxes. Two benefits,1.
more boxes, and 2. I know where to get the best ice cream after my next
outing

It's interesting where we get our ideas, isn't it? I finally took Paul

Adirondacks series ready to plant that are all animals found in the

travel so I could just leave a little something behind without having
to travel with all my carving stuff.
