We were first finders for Lock Wench's Leprechaun's Revenge, a series
of 7 teeny-tiny (and we do mean extra wee!) boxes that stretch from
DeWitt to Utica. (Lock Wench, we fear that the one at the Indian Casino
is MIA, as 6 pairs of eyes couldn't spot it on or near the black
poles). If you hunt for them, think button-sized...

We also made a side trip to the Fenner Windmill Farm, which is a very
nice location.

This morning, before kids started climbing on me, I carved yet another
nice stamp, and may plant it today en route to Enchanted Forest water
park.

Paul in SF
