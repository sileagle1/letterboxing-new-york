WNY Gathering theme change!

From: Daina | Date: 2005-07-06 23:06:00 UTC
Do NOT be alarmed! Your boxes are still good! :-)

Actually, I recently realized I had gotten my "Just Get Out There and
Play" theme from having heard Mark & Sue Pepe's "Just Get Out There
and Box" theme some time ago (which they have REALLY cool looking
wristbands for that you'll all be able to purchase at the
gathering!). I apologized profusely to him a few weeks ago, but he
was totally cool with our using that theme.

However, I'm thinking other folks might not be as understanding of
what looks like a blatant ripoff. Plus, I like to think of myself as
a creative individual without the guilt of piggybacking (intentional
or not), so I changed the theme to "Just Playin' Around WNY" instead.
Still the same type of boxes, now with 100% less guilt and shame
free! :-)

We've got nearly 50 folks planning on stopping by in August, with a
god 25 boxes in the park and even more close by...have you signed up
yet???

People don't care :) We understand that there was no "swipe" of
themes. We started with that idea, and it was fresh and clean, and
we should stay with it. Boxing is a tight group that worries not of
small insignificant things such as trivial closeness of thoughts.
That is what makes us great. Truly.
We grow, as a group, on the ability to "steal" good
ideas/thoughts/themes. This is what keeps our game solid. We need
no worries what the other person thinks, just the glamour of an
original thought being rethought without the knowledge of being
brought up first. It is all good, just restated later.
