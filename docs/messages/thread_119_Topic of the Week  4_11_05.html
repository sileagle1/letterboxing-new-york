As a matter of fact, I went to one this weekend! Sprite and
Highlander organized the "T.G.F.T.D. Mini-meet" (Thank Ghod For Team
Dakota) to honor a letter-boxing family that has recently left the
area. After a hot breakfast and and several exchanges, I hit the trails.

Cup of Joe: Alive and well.

Lonesome Train: Alive and well. A brilliant hide, I almost missed it.

Erie Passage: Alive and well.

Squirrel's Treat: Alive and well.

Whoooo's There?: Alive and well, replaced torn baggie. Another
brilliant hide!

What a Drag!: Status Unknown. When I got to the site, another couple
was there taking pictures. I asked "By any chance are you
letterboxing?" They replied "No, but you're too late, the other team
got here first." I responded that it wasn't that kind of a contest, I
didn't have to be the first one there, I just had to sign in. The guy
answered "You're not going to be able to sign in, the other team took
the tupperware box with them." It was the word 'tupperware' that
filled me with dread. I searched the location and couldn't find the
box. I decided that they had taken the box, so the couple wouldn't
walk off with it, so I came back after I had found all the other boxes
in the area, but it still wasn't there. I can only assume it was
another boxer from the mini-meet. The couple said it was a white
mini-van. Anyone have any idea who this might be?

Drew the Cary Tree: Probably gone. There was a storm in the area
April 2-3. A lot of trees and power lines got knocked down. When I
got to the site, it looked like a bomb hit it. When I walked in the
gate, I saw that the maintenance crew had logged and stacked several
fallen trees. When I saw that one log was hollow, I got a really bad
feeling. I followed the clues which led me right to a tree stump. No
box. I went over to the stack of logs and searched in case the crew
hadn't noticed the box. No box. I checked the other stacks of logs.
No box. I went over to the trash barrel and discovered it was well
used by considerate dog-owners (work on it! :D) I didn't go digging,
but I couldn't see anything that looked like tupperware either and I
could see pretty much everything in the barrel. This one may be gone!

Lost Nearby: Alive and well.

Food Lion: Enlighten??? Enlighten!!! Another brilliant hide! Alive
and well.

I apologize for revealing an indication of where Drew the Cary Tree
may (be/have been) hidden, but I truly believe that it's gone, and I'm
hoping that a previous finder can verify this. I've gone over my
sit-rep and made sure that there are no clues to locations in any of
the other boxes. And yes, I'm aware that you won't be able to get
clues to all of these boxes on either Atlasquest or LBNA, I consider
that to be part of the game.
