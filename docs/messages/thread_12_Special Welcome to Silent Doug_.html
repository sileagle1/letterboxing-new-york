Special Welcome to Silent Doug!

From: paulonthepark | Date: 2004-09-29 23:16:50 UTC
I just noticed that Silent Doug silently joined our Central NY group.

Rather than let him slip in silent and unnoticed, I thought I should
tell you that we're honored by his presence. Silent Doug is a very
knowledgable resource for letterboxing and has an excellent and
informative web site devoted to letterboxing.

You can find it at http://www.letterboxing.info/

The site has great information throughout it. I found it to be very
helpful when I began on my fast start letterboxing training program.
I see that he's now added even more. You'll find tips on getting
started, stamp carving for kids, how to plan a gathering,
letterboxing history, and much, much more!

AND...(drum roll, please)... you'll want to check out all the great
letterboxing fashions (T-shirts to boxer shorts, and even dog T-
shirts), plus bumper stickers and other neat gifts to buy your Uncle
Paul (or your favorite letterboxers) for birthdays or the holiday
season!

Please visit Silent Doug's site!

So, Silent Doug, thanks for joining LbCNY - glad to have you with us!

Paul in SF
