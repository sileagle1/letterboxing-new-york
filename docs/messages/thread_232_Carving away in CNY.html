Carving away in CNY

From: Paul | Date: 2005-07-24 22:53:10 UTC
Let's see... I've been in CNY a little over 24 hours, and have carved
a large hinged stamp for a new box soon to be planted
called, "Fifteen Years". You'll have to answer a number of questions
correctly in order to locate the box.

I've also prepared replacements for the missing "Trip, Trap" box and
the missing Oriskany namesake mini-box. I also carved a really nice
stamp that I'm sending down to my friend in Key West to plant. I may
re-carve Drillmaster, too, time allowing. I went to find "The Place
of the Nettles" (my niece's and friends box). We're organizing a
family letterboxing extravaganza outing this week to Moss Island,
Trenton Falls, the Herkimer Home, and some swimming holes [GRIN].
There's also talk of getting out to Green Lakes to see how many of
catbead's boxes we can find.

I'm planning to have "Fifteen Years" in place during the week, so
that anyone coming in for my get-together on Saturday can swoop in on
it if desired. It'll be about 3 miles away from here.

When not carving, I've been in the swimming pool. Thanks for having
the super-heat turned down before I arrived. That was very
thoughtful -- and I'm happy to report that I haven't gotten a
mosquito bite yet!

Paul in SF (currently in Oriskany, NY)
