Walmart Mini-Scrapbook Alert

From: catbead1 | Date: 2005-11-01 19:34:31 UTC
Walmart has a seasonal display in the crafts
section of the mini-scrapbooks, 3 3/4" x 3 3/4"
spiral bound, cardstock wt. pages for 94¢. These
are the same little books sometimes found in
Michaels Crafts. While they only have 13 pages,
the cardstock is heavy enough to be stamped on
both sides, and fit nicely into the smaller
square Lock & Lock's (think sandwich w/the crust
trimmed off).

Cheers,
catbead

From: sahalie_8 | Date: 2005-11-16 13:21:12 UTC
-Catbead, thanks for the tip on mini-scrapbooks. These work great for
hitchhikers and postals also. I just stocked up on these yesterday.
Here's a tip, if you carefully pry open the prongs just slightly and
remove the back & front cover, you can add a few pages from another
book. I split one to make two others with 19/20 pages. You can then
cut new pages from cardstock and use a hole puncher for the top holes
and make a whole new book with the leftover covers and spiral. Then
push the prongs back into place. (Just be careful not to pry prongs
open too much).
Sahalie
