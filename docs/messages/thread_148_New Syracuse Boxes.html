I have planted another Syracuse series...this one called "Trio of
Visually Impaired Rodents". Some of you local partiers might even
recognize the planted area. *grin*

I am finding that although I love finding new boxes and collecting the
stamps..I am even crazier about making the boxes and hiding them. I've
gotten so many great ideas from more experienced letterboxers that I am
now going a bit daffy collecting materials for new boxes. My son looked
at my most recent receipt from a supply jaunt and commented...."So is
film school not in my future afterall?" LOL

I was given a challenge by Paul in SF for a box design..and I am
pursuing that as well. Stay tuned, CNY boxers!
