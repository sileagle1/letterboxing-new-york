His Dark Materials update...

From: thespiritofmrpigeon | Date: 2005-06-19 22:07:19 UTC
Grrr.... We went out to Rice Creek Field Station this morning to
check on our series. We'd received word yesterday that a couple of
our boxes had been vandalized by -of all people- geocachers! The
first one that was struck was easily repaired and should be alright
in it's current location. Kaisa's box (#3 in the series) has been
removed. We need to replace it in a different location, and we'll do
so in the near future. What the geocachers wrote and drew into
Kaisa's logbook was completely OBSCENE and I'm just thankful that no
families had attempted to find it. We owe a BIG thank you to
Trailhoundz for letting us know what had happened to our boxes! We
knew that geocachers had found Kaisa's box in the past, but this was
ridiculous! We'll post again when we hide this box in a new location.
Grrrrr......

BTW, Trailhoundz has an awesome signature stamp!!!! Dandelion and I
both loved it.

Dear Crafty Mouse -
On behalf of all geocachers, I apologize. Most geocachers, (we are
also geocachers) are pretty nice. Most have no interest in
letterboxing (not high tech enough), but they wouldn't think of
bothering a letterbox if they ran into one. I am so disappointed and I

another spot. Scout (Miller family)

From: thespiritofmrpigeon | Date: 2005-06-22 15:41:57 UTC
Thanks everyone for all the kind thoughts and words about the
geocaching incident. I'm done being miffed about it and we'll just
move it to a new location. We took a vacation to Kingston Ontario this
weekend and did some serious letterboxing! (Finding tons of boxes
ALWAYS cheers you up, right?!) We had a great time and highly
recommend the Kingston area boxes! All very well done and mostly quick
and easy finds. Lots of fun historic places too. (Norasta, you guys
are GOOD!)

Now all I can think about is what I'm going to carve for the WNY
gathering! So many ideas..... We hope to see you all there!

Man, I think I left my brain in Canada. It's really hard to sit here
at my desk at work when I know there are more boxes out there to find
on such a nice day!

Craftymouse
