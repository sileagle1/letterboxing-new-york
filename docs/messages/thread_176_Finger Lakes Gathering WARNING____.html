WARNING!

Attendees at The Finger Lakes LB Gathering of June 11, 2005 may have
gone home with more than they bargained for - the Ithaca area is known
to be a breeding ground for numerous pesky critters. Check your
personal belongings! These four were last seen in Stewart Park:

Cayuga Cootie - picked up near the shores of Cayuga Lake or in nearby
gorges. Swims, crawls, flies and is an Olympic class jumper.

Ithacootie - frequents the Ithaca Commons, shopping malls and flea
markets. Lurks on ebay; has a Gold Mastercard.

Cornellie Cootie - this lovely cootie is well educated and loves the
campus ice cream. Graduate degree in hematology.

Finger LaCootie - found around any of the Finger Lakes, particularly
virulent near the numerous wineries. Bilingual, speaks Cootese and
Fleasian.
