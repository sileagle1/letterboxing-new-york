Dogs Welcome at Desperately Seeking Sun

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-10-12 12:28:30 UTC
I am happy to announce that the staff at Oneida Shores County Park
will allow letterboxers to bring their dogs. Besides allowing leashed
dogs on the trails, they will allow dogs to be in the lodge with us as
we gather for the Desperately Seeking Sun event!

Obviously dog owners will be responsible for their pets and should be
aware that we expect about 100 people at this event, many of them
children. If you think your pet might be skittish with so much
activity, please rethink bringing him/her. But if you have a dog who
does well in crowds and around other dogs, please feel free to bring
your pet!

I am hoping to encourage boxers who haven't attended in the past
because they did not want to leave their dogs at home.
