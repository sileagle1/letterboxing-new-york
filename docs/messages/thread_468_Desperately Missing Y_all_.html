From: Daina | Date: 2006-02-20 22:34:41 UTC
I think it'll be about the same as having the big USA board and the
smaller state/regional boards...it'll streamline the amount of info
LBNY gets so that we're not getting local boxing news, just the stuff
that will affect the state in general. When I have something I know
is important to more than just WNY (like gatherings or such), I
always crosspost. If it's just something about boxing in Knowlesville
or Pembroke on the weekend where I know only a WNY person could get
here on short notice, I figure it's not something anyone else is
really going to miss. :-)

Plus, with the smaller boards, I usually hit them when we're going to
visit the area (ex--we check out the Texas boards right before we
head down for Thanksgiving), so we don't miss anything.
