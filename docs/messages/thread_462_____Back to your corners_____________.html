Well......I've been thinking about all this shtuff, and I am gonna
toss in my 43 cents.....and....maybe, yes, piss some people off, but
hey.....that's me. I have to say that I think Lock Wench hit on
something (unknowingly, perhaps) that I think we should, again, take
to heart. Head to your own corners :) Huh? Yhea, why can't there
be a LbWNY and LbCNY again?? I'd like for Phinster to start a LbENY
site. There is more than enough people there to do so. Why the hell
isn't there a LbNYC site? There is a huge pool of people AND boxes
there. LbLI is waiting to happen on the island, just as LbADK seems
to be waiting up in the big hills to our north. Same, same with
LbCAT (or whatever the Catskills would be called).

It is noble to want to be unified under one roof, and now we are :)
Sometimes boundries, borders and lines are drawn/placed/occur
naturally for the good of all. This allows for relationships and
rivalries (both good) to develope. I love the fact that we all want
to hug as NYers, but we aren't all the same. We (I sure do) need
our own space, as well as being identified as something bigger.

Listen, New York state is broken into regions for many, many
reasons. We can, and should keep LbNY, but regionality is what
makes us all great (Is it soda or is it Pop?). The LbWNY and CNY
sites never should have gone away. We the people (that's the
teacher in me) should have simply made a new site. There are
PLENTY of other places that have 1, 2, 3 sites that cover the same
place. Maine has a site as Conn. Both are under the LbNE. Who
cares if there is more sites. To me it's just more fun to hear
about people being out Lbing. *shrug*

Central Ohio has it's own site. Western Maryland too. Think about
NYS tourism.....There is a Dept/Board/Office for the whole state,
more for all the regions, further more for each of the counties, and
then cities have their own. Nobody complains. How often do we
cross-post on different sites for Lbing???? I check Lb sites from
all over the country. I like to hear about the different ideas.

YES!!! We should all head to our corners, and dig what we have. It
isn't going to make us a lesser boxing state, in fact it will spruce
us all up. I'd join ANY group in NY. I am in Saratoga (I have a
box planted there with another to come in the spring *Thanks
In'laws*) all the time. To me that's Hello LbENY for me. My
brother In-law is on the Island, and I'm in NYC constantly. I'd
join those too. I'm headed to the ADKs next week :)

Thanks to Sprite, we HAVE a Design/Logo/emblem for LbNY. I look
forward to having it on a patch. It looks great, and has already
been agreed/voted/decided on a time ago.

Folks, this is a HOBBY. It is supposed to be fun. I, for one,
believe that there is nothing wrong with "being sent to our
corners". I kinda like the idea :)

I don't always agree with what is correct, but I will always stand
up for what is right.


-WINK

From: Cyndie | Date: 2006-02-12 19:18:16 UTC
Urban Boxing group? What an awesome idea! Urban boxing is almost an
entirely new animal. No conventional trails to follow, few forests,
etc. You have to be stealthy and there are a whole lot of different
hazards. Instead of a squirrel taking your container...how about a
homeless man? It's happened to me!

I've really enjoyed hiding my urban boxes and am looking forward to
hunting out those NYC boxes.

I think the "official" patch should have the typeface look like
graffitti and have a subway running across it..just kidding! Sheesh! :)
