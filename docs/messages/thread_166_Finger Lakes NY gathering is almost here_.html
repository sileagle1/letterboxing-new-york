Subject: Finger Lakes NY gathering is almost here!

Thinking about coming to the Finger Lakes Gathering in Ithaca, NY next
Saturday, June 11th from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m.? We've already heard from more
than sixty attendees-to-be representing at least six states and one foreign
country (O, Canada!) and have plenty of room for more.

New boxes have been sprouting up all over the place along with the
wildflowers and weeds around here, so there's more reason than ever to
visit even if you've been to Ithaca before. Word on the street (and in the
towers) is that one particular Tompkins County mystery box will be slightly
more available than usual on the day of the gathering, and it's one you
don't want to miss.

If you're interested, many new details have been posted here:

http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=2

As always, we'd be happy to answer any questions about the gathering and
the Ithaca area, so feel free to ask.

Hoping to see many of you next weekend,

TurtleMcQ and Mama Bear

All of my boxes (and Mama Bear's, I think) are already posted on Atlas
Quest. I'm not sure about the rest of the ones I mentioned on the event
listing as being nearby, though, since they were planted by other people.
If you're coming from afar, do a search on Tompkins County, NY at LBNA.
Those will be the boxes within relatively easy driving distance of the
gathering. Anything outside of Tompkins County will be at least a 20 minute
drive away.

I can't double-check right now since both sites are still down, but
definitely feel free to ask if you have questions about where boxes are in
the area (except those mystery boxes - you're on your own there!). If
you're a AAA member, get an Ithaca/Tompkins County map. It will help a lot.

From: themama_bear | Date: 2005-06-09 16:28:02 UTC
This is exactly the kind of thing done at a gathering! This will be a
great opportunity to meet all the people whose stamps you have seen and
exchange stamps in your logbooks. Frankly, we've really been hoping to
meet the infamous Maggie Miller who always seems to get to every box
and stamp in before us! That's one of the main focuses for this
gathering - getting to gather and meet each other. I would venture a
guess that there will also be some new letterboxes planted in the area
and the gathering will be an opportunity to gather clues for these
boxes, as well.

I hope you all decide to come. Given that we've seen your stamp in
almost every local box we've been to, it just wouldn't be the same
without you!
