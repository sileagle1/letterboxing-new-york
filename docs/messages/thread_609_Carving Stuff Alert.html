Carving Stuff Alert

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-10-15 12:10:46 UTC
After much nagging, the Syracuse University Bookstore on Waverly (in
the Student Center) has finally restocked the pink stuff in the handy
6 x 12 inch size. Although I am a PZ Cut fan, it's nice to know this
material is available in the area when I am in a pinch. Each of these
large sheets costs 13.85 and SU employees and staff get a 10%
discount. Remember, Speedy STAMP = pink stuff = GOOD. Speedy CUT =
crumbly stuff = BAD.

They also have the small blocks of pink stuff, Speedball handles and
blades, master carve in both sizes and thicknesses and assorted
carvable erasers. Also the Moleskin 3 packs of blank logbooks as well
as the other sizes. Plenty of the 3 x 5 blank sketchbooks/logbooks as
well as a rather large selection of sketchbooks/journals, both in
spirals and bound versions.

The hardest thing to do is find a parking spot. I recommend avoiding
days when SU football/basketball is playing a home game, unless you
are coming up for the game anyway! The SU Bookstore IS open on
weekends. Also, there are now 3 boxes planted on campus, and another
12 within a mile. And they are NOT all mine! You could make a day of
it. :)
