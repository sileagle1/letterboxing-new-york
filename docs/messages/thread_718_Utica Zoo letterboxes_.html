Hi everyone!

For those of you interested in the Utica Zoo letterboxes (series of
12), I wanted to let you know about some updates. The Prehistoric
Pokilotherms box had disappeared last fall, around late September or
so. The stamp in the Quilled Rodents box had cracked in half, but was
still usable, if you creatively used your imagination! This Friday,
April 18, I will be replacing the entire PP box, and the stamp in the
QR box. Thank you to those of you who have shown interest in visiting
and supporting the zoo!

By the way, there's a neat event planned at the Utica Zoo this
Saturday for Earth Day. It's worth the trip to visit the zoo, get 12
stamps, and enjoy some Earth Day activities up at the zoo! I hope
some of you can make it!

I've got the boxes listed on both Atlas Quest and LB.org but I think
the clues are easier to read on AQ.

Thanks~
