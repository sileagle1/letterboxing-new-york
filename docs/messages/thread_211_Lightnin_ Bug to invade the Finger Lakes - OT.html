Don't have any quick LB's to suggest but your non -LB wife might want to look into an all natural
"bug away" type of repellant - one that contains only essential oils and alcohol - in a pump spray
bottle. Works wonderfully on mosquitoes, black flies and deer flies (as well as other pesky little
bugs, gnats etc). What makes these types of sprays nice is they don't contain deet and aren't
smelly and sticky like conventional ones. hence no need to wash off body before retiring for the
evening. Have a good LBing weekend.
