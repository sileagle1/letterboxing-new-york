Boxing in Cayuga & Western Onondaga Counties

From: Jen Z | Date: 2006-07-18 20:11:15 UTC
I guess I should have written this when I posted about my Skaneateles boxes... and certainly not meant to be a "downer"... but anyone searching for letterboxes in the area should be aware that there have been several cases lately of foxes acting out aggressively -and assumed to have rabies- in Cayuga and western Onondaga Counties. Currently all the animals that have attacked people have been Red foxes and Gray Foxes-- in the areas of Sennett (area outside of Skaneateles and Auburn), Fleming, Niles, and yesterday three people were attacked by a Gray Fox in the village of Weedsport, which is near Port Byron and Jordan.

Be alert when you're boxing in the woods and if you box with your pups, make sure they are up-to-date on their Rabies vaccinations. And if you see any animals acting strange, slobbering, walking drunkenly, acting aggressive, or out during the day when they are a nocturnal species...make a mad dash out of there.

You may also want to carry your carving tools with you for protection (okay, just kidding on that one!).

No reason to stop boxing, but one more reason to be alert when out in the trail.
