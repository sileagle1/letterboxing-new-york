Thanksgiving Weekend Mini Meet

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-11-12 11:45:05 UTC
Don't know what to do with those relatives after the holiday? Need
something to do that everyone from the grandkids to Old Uncle Ernie
can enjoy? Why not give them all a store bought stamp and a notepad
and bring them to our mini gathering?

We finally have a date, time and meeting place! On Friday (the day
after Thanksgiving) at 10 am, the Oneida Community Mansion House in
Oneida NY has graciously offered LBNY the use of their lovely "lobby"
room for this event. If you have never been to this wonderful National
Historic Landmark, you are really in for a treat. You can read more
about the mansion in the Taphophiles, Unite! - Oneida Community
Mansion House letterbox clues.

The Mansion House is not charging us for the use of their room for our
"exchange" gathering, which is incredible generous as they usually
charge several hundred dollars for this lovely HUGE room with
fireplace. But if you want to find the special letterboxes hidden
WITHIN the Mansion, you will have to take the tour and gather up the
clues! Adults are $5, students are $3 and kids under 12 are FREE. If
we get enough people to sign up, they will consider giving us a
special discount rate! Now you can't beat that! If we get a huge
number of people, we will run another group at the 2 pm tour.

You can still come to the gathering and do exchanges...plus find the
letterbox that is hidden in a graveyard on the property. But I hope
you will support this wonderful facility by taking the tour. It's
absolutely fascinating and just seeing the amazing silk braided
"paintings" of Jessie Catherine Kinsley, a member of the Community, is
well worth the price of admission. They even have a special tour
designed just for kids!

So pack up your holiday leftovers ( turkey sandwiches, anyone?) and
come join us for some exchanges and special boxes. We'll meet at the
front of the Mansion House at 9:30 am (although you can park out back
where there is a lot more room for cars) and we'll begin by taking the
tour promptly at 10 am. After the tour we'll meet up in the Lobby Room
for some exchanges, our packed box lunches and the event stamp. Door
Prizes will also be given out. EVERYONE WHO TAKES THE TOUR GETS A DOOR
PRIZE! After this you are free to roam the grounds to find the
Taphophiles Unite! letterbox, or go after the many new boxes in the area.

Won't you come and join us and meet your local CNY boxers? There are
many new boxers in the area so this should be a lot of fun. Everyone
is welcome to attend however ....bring your visiting relatives..they
don't have to be a boxer to come take the tour and hang out with us!
Come near, come far...just don't come late! :)

Please sign up by Wednesday November 15th so we have a more accurate
headcount, as they will be bringing in special tour guides for us
depending on the numbers and we might earn a discount rate! You can
still sign up after that or even show up the day of the event, but I
won't guarantee you can go on the tour, get the special inside boxes
and earn a letterboxing door prize unless you sign up by the 15th. So
please help us out by signing up as early as possible! Thanks!

All you have to bring is a some bucks for the tour ( a bargain), a box lunch if you want to eat there too, and that's it!

Kate Mockler, the curator there, is very excited to have us, and I think it will be a great time for all. Best part is..it's weather proof!  We have nearly 40 people signed up, so not exactly a mini-meet anymore, but the fact that there are so many new people will make this an excellent time!

Also, it's great practice for some of the bigger gathers, where things are a lot busier with more activities and boxes. This will be a great time to meet other boxers, do some exchanges, and get a few boxes that are not available to the casual boxer, as the clues are not listed online and you have to do the tour to get the clues!

Anyone who has family who just wants to join us for the tour and bring a box lunch is welcome to come as well. So get those relatives off the couch and have them work off a little of that Thanksgiving dinner!

RSVPs for the tour are due by tomorrow, Wed, the 15th. You can still come if you aren't signed up by then but I can't guarantee a tour or a door prize or the special clues if you don't sign up.
