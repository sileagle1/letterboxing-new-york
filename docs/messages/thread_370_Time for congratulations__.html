Thank you so much, Buggylou! As a matter of fact, I just posted my
100th plant today. It was accomplished not without a lot of
scrambling! You see, my goal was to plant 100 traditional
letterboxes..no hitchhikers, personal stamps, PLB's, cooties, etc.
included in this 100. (Although I do have some of those out there,too!)
The 100 boxes each had to have a handcarved stamp carved by me only.
Each had to be planted by me as well. I thought I had hit the 100 mark
a few days ago only to realize that one of my boxes was actually
planted for me by Sprite..so I didn't count it. Did I mention that I
wanted to accomplish this goal in 6 months? *grin* I made it, with a
few weeks to spare. WHEW!

But the best part to me is that out of these 100 boxes, 68 are planted
in unique areas...meaning that the majority of them are not
series....but individual places to visit. I has SUCH a blast going to
all these cool places..and I hope my fellow boxers will too. The
majority of them are planted in Onondaga County, and it is my aim that
people will visit this beautiful area and enjoy it as I do.

Thanks so much for your offer of congratulations. I would especially
like to thank Catbead, my letterboxing mentor..who taught me much about
carving and especially planting. Now it's time for me to do
maintenance and replace those earlier boxes that went missing! LOL

Lock Wench
