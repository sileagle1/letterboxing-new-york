Hi to all in this group,
Me and my dog budster have done a lot of letterboxing in the past 5
or 6 years but now has slowed down a bit and has left it to me and my
better half* . (99% me)
------------------------------------------------------------------
My computer is set up to respond to e-mails at my juno address and
rarely is it in internet browsing mode. (so linking is a several step
process for me)
---------------------------------------------------------------
Concerning my letterboxing habits:
Mostly concentrating on placing ("P" ing) but should be doing more
finding ( "F" ing ) so J* tells me. So when I get things in order
around here we will be looking to stamp in more , but for now things
are on hold.

Would be interested in meeting up and discuss the "letterbox nature
of things" though. Anywhere within a 50 mile radius of Kingston ,NY.
Please keep me posted on up coming events and we will get everyone
out there to meet "the budster"


Happy Letterboxing.......Budster of the Catskills
