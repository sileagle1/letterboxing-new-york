Just a repeat of my box request from the WNY group...any takers?

With summer finally starting, I'm getting into party prep mode for
the gatherings (WNY in August ESPECIALLY)! However, unlike last year,
where we local folks really put out quite a spread for our visitors,
I haven't really heard from many of your about donating a box for the
theme. So, lemme see if I can rope some of you in and explain what
we're trying to do at the same time! :-)

Basically, we want to encourage more folks to visit (more people
means more cool stamps and exchanges to see!), so we try to put out
as many boxes as possible and leave them out. I'm finding more people
from out of town are interested in tackling the Royalton Ravine
series than the nearby Medina boxes because they know they'll get a
bunch of boxes in a row without having to drive all over in
unfamiliar territory. Plus, at the gatherings, most of us just want
to hang out and box the immediate area, which makes a series in the
park even better.

However, it's a lot of carving for one sprite and a guy with a sword,
so we need your help. We'd like at least 10 boxes that fit with the
recreational theme we've got and I think we've got a do-able series.
How about Monopoly? It's something simple we can all add to and if we
each tried to carve one space (Park Place, B & O Railroad, etc.) we'd
definitely have enough to make a decent series that has yet to have
been done anywhere else. The Highlander and I have no problem hiding
the boxes and writing up the clues for the weekend, but we need more
hands to carve.

(gets in front of the waving banner of LBNA and salutes the troops)
So now, men (and women and various and asundry boxing
pets/children)...NOW is the time for all of us to band together to
defend the honor of this great lan...er, REGION of ours! Will we let
the state of New York forever be called New York City by outsiders
that don't know any better...or will our boxes stand proud and true,
designating us forever as upstate New York, the land of the Finger
Lakes and the rushing Niagara waters (where the dairy farm smell is
plentiful, and crime is mostly limited to people snagging our boxes
and throwing them out)?

WHO'S WITH ME?????!?!?!?

(charges out along with a Speedball in one hand and a bag of Dollar
Tree erasers in the other)

Seriously, anyone that would like to add boxes to the Monopoly series
(or any other game/sport/playtime related boxes, contact us offlist.
Thanks!
