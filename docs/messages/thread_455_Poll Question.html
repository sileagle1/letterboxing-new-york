The poll question asks whether you think we should design a patch...not
for an opinion on what the patch should look like. Please vote.

The next step would be to place many different designs to be voted
upon. (including of course the t-shirt design that has been mentioned
should the designer wish to create and/or design another that would be
great too!).

The poll probably should not have used the word "logo"...sorry about
that. A "logo" generally sticks for a very long time changing slowly
over the years. We may wish to rethink the design in another 2 years as
new people join LbNY!

Finally, as a State, we are all ready depicted in the Northeast
patch...most patches are of regional types (i.e. Northeast, Mid-
Atlantic, Pacific North-west, etc). We may be doing something 'unique
by doing our own state!

From: Daina | Date: 2006-02-08 21:15:17 UTC
I know this sounds silly (since it makes more sense to be democratic
about this), but I would kinda hope that we'd keep the logo, if only
as a final nod to the meager efforts of the old LBWNY group. Part of
the whole plan of becoming a unified group began with making this t-
shirt logo reflect the whole state (originally, it was a WNY logo). I
know it made life a lot easier for most, but disbanding LBWNY was
actually a much harder choice to make than I thought (and actually
affected more people than I thought), since the old group was really
more like a bunch of loosely connected local buddies more than
anything. In retrospect, though, it's been nice seeing us all
together under one banner.

Anyway, I guess I'm a little sentimental, but I'd like to think that
a little something from that big merge was still left of us crazy
Western NY folks! :-) As in the past, however, I'll certainly
understand if it's not what the consensus wants.

The S & H
