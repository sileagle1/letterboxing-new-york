Paul in SF will be in NYC at end of month and other news!

From: Paul | Date: 2006-03-15 11:54:52 UTC
Hi folks!
It's been a while since I've posted here, hasn't it?

I was in Utica this past weekend for the funeral of my extended
family "Mom", a truly wonderful, loving woman. Almost 1,000 people
showed up at the wake, which speaks volumes for how she touched many
lives (including mine).

Anyway, I was VERY amused that it was warm and snow-free in central
NY, while at the same time, San Francisco was cold enough to have a
dusting of something resembling white stuff, but more like ice
crystals. Repeat after me and our leader, Dubya, "There is no such
thing as global warming...".

While in Utica, I did scout out a potential box planting site for
when I return in July. I plan on spending about 10 days in Utica-
Rome in July, and I think we'll throw together a gathering/LbNY 2nd
anniversary celebration.

I'll also run another 'Creative Thinking for Letterboxers' workshop,
like I did here in SF. It was a big success, lots of fun, too, and
we generated hundred of ideas for new & creative letterboxes. (Well,
some of them, Lock Wench had already beat us to, but anyway...)

BUT, this month, I have a resceduled one week vacation in the Big
Apple. I was originally supposed to go last fall. This time, it's
for real.

SO, I'm going to prepare a box or maybe two to plant in Manhattan,
AND if Miss Moon is available, we can do out meet & exchange
finally. (Miss Moon, are you there? What about getting together?)

If anyone else in the area would like to connect up with the
infamous LbNY founder and bicoastal letterboxer, let me know, OK?

I'm in Manhattan for the full last week of March. I'll replant my
niece's MIA box, too.)

For those with curious minds, you can see what I've been up to by
checking out my blog, "Paul Is In SF, Yes!" at
http://pgonyea.blogspot.com where you'll see some of my digital art
projects, hear me rant about our government a bit, and also find
some very useful fun things.

LB'ing hugs all around,
Paul in SF
