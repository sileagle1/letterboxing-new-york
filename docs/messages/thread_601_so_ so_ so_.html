Miss Buggy and I went out and got the rest of the 'trail' boxes from
Wonders today. We did skip the Overlook drive-by as the Overlook was
majorly crowded, but the trail boxes were well worth the return trip.
As a bonus, we ran into Team Safari out on the trail. We boxed
together for a while, then they had to head home while we grabbed the
last few boxes. A few muggles along the way, but the boxes from #11
on all seemed safe and sound as of this afternoon.

Out on the autumn trail, letterboxing with my daughter..... what
better way to spend what may be the last warmish day we get for quite
a while? :-)
