Oh Catbead what a good idea! I love the idea of my dogs having their own stamps and logbooks! LOL!
 
I just started Letterboxing a month or so ago but have brought my 2 retired racing greyhounds with me every time I've searched and/or planted. Lucy and Clifford LOVE to go for walks so it seemed like a natural extension... another fun activity to do with my pups!  I carry extra water, a foldable water bowl, canine-safe bug spray, and poo-bags in a special backpack along with my letterbox stuff.
 
The "kids" make great hiking companions and are generally game for anything.  Clifford is my big fawn boy--he loves to walk out front and "navigate". My little girl Lucy lays down and "guards" the backpack while I work on stamping the logbooks. Being a single woman and wandering the boonies looking for stamps--just feels comfortable for me to have 2 big muscular, athletic animals along with me. :o) In fact, one of the reasons I took up Letterboxing was because it was something I could do with the pups--they are such an important part of my life and I always include them in everything I can! :o)
