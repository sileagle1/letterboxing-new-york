New LbCNY Moderators

From: Paul | Date: 2005-07-18 23:46:57 UTC
I'd like to welcome 2 LbCNY members to the role of co-moderators:
Lock Wench & MtNestRobin, who will share the duties of moderator,
with me as a distant back-up.

Catbead, who has been a superb moderator for the group, has decided
to step down for health and personal reasons. Libby, thank you once
again for your wonderful work as LbCNY's moderator. We REALLY
appreciate it!

We have reason to celebrate - it's LbCNY's First Anniversary! It may
not be the biggest letterboxing group around, but it's a darn good
one! And it's grown considerably. When I planted my first boxes in
NY, I had no idea that there were others nearby ready to jump in.

Congratulationms LbCNY members for a successful first year of
connecting with each other & letterboxing!

Please join me in welcoming MtNestRobin and Lock Wench as new co-
moderators!

Paul in SF
