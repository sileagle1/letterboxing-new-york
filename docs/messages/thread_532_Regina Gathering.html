Hello Fellow Letterboxers!



As you may already know, I am planning a Letterboxing Gathering for
August 6th (which is also my birthday!) in Regina, Saskatchewan. My
home-town of Regina is a beautiful place I miss it a lot and can't
wait to get back there for a visit. Thank you so much to anyone
willing to consider carving a stamp for the first ever letterboxing
gathering in the province of Saskatchewan, and the first ever box
plantings in the capital city. I am so excited to be seeding this
area, and am so grateful to any carvers willing to donate boxes to be
planted here. THANK YOU! I'm going to guess that most of you have
never had the chance to visit Regina, and probably a few of you have
never even HEARD of Regina, so I thought I'd put together a bit of
info about the town in order to inspire your stamp carving!



First, here is a Wiki entry all about Regina!

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Regina%2C_Saskatchewan



Next, here are various links that address interesting things that
might be good for carving ideas. They are in no particular order.
Have fun browsing! If you do decide to pick a particular item, could
you drop me a line and let me know what general kind of stamp you'll
be making, as I will try and carve a few myself and don't want to
duplicate too much.
