From: Pamela Smith | Date: 2006-09-22 12:18:52 UTC
The actual badge (Hi Tech Hide & Seek) is for older girls.  It's an Interest Project Award for girls 11-17 and does include several activities. My daughter just became a Junior and at there are badges that touch on the subject - using a compass, hiking, etc - but nothing for LBing or G-caching directly.

A group could do one on their own, but I think you're only supposed to have one "Our Own Troop's" badge that reflects an interest of the whole group. I suppose local groups could come up with a patch, which is different than a badge, but I'm not sure of the 'rules' on patches.
