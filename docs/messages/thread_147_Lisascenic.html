I don't include stamp pads in my boxes, but I would IF I thought the
stamp should be a particular color.

Here in the Bay Area, the boxes that are up on Mt. Tamalpais have
their own pads... and in 1 instance, the stamp is of red lips, like a
kiss, so a red stamp pad is in the box.

If you include a stamp pad, make sure it's sealed as watertight as
possible (or your whole box will be inked). You may want to include
some extra paper towels as a courtesy.
