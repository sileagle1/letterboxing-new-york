From: tamsynboxer | Date: 2006-10-12 21:16:31 UTC
Greetings,

I don't know about the other organizers, but I didn't say anything to the boxers featured in
the article about the consequences -- mostly because I'm sure they didn't mean to cause
trouble, as well as the fact that there isn't going to be another article in our area immediately
(or a gathering, for that matter). We are still in "negotiations" with the park over how many
boxes we will be able to leave as permanent plants. Certainly, if they are going to charge us
the (geocaching) fee of $25/box, it will be few if any, squashing our hopes of making this
park a letterboxing destination.

Also, I personally shy away from personal publicity, but was disappointed to see newbies
featured in this article instead of some of my Wonders cohorts, who have more breadth of
experience and history than these boxers who started in May or June.

Now, what do you think about a television story on letterboxing? (Ducking from the can of
worms this may open.) Same concerns, just a larger scale?

It was great to see so many friends -- old and new -- this weekend! Thanks for making it so
much fun.

Tamsyn
