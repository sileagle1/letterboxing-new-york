Re: [LbNY] Re: Genesee Country Museum

From: vicki crozzoli | Date: 2006-11-17 21:29:47 UTC
I just called the Genesee Country museum.  Heres the deal.  There are 3 boxes in the nature center.  They are doing an intro to letterboxing at 2pm, then the teacher will walk you thru and show you how to follow the clues to find the first box.  Then if you want to find the others you can do it on your own.
 
The historic village is closed for the season.  The nature center is open all year round. 
Admission is free for members.  $4 for adults and $3 for kids.
 
I think we are going to go and give it a try....would love to see some of you and do some exchanges!!
