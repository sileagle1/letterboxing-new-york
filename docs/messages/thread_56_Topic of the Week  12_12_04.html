> How did you come up with your trail name and stamp?

My trail name is my Netscape identity, and a perfect fit. The stamp was easy, we had a
crazy cat (Edward) full of personality who lived to the ripe age of 18, in spite of numerous
health problems, and was happy up until the end. He was long and lean with with looong
silky fur and a huge plume of a tail. Edward loved making an entrance strutting into the
room, his tail feathers fluffed out to their fullest. We'd see the plume go by and a few
minutes later it'd go by again...but we'd never see him leave the room, only enter. He
loved tuna (or any seafood catfood) so whenever it was served all we had to call out was
"arrrr" and he'd come running. And the cat had a smile on his face. The image on my
stamp is Edward running to the call of "arrrr" inside a waivering line circle "bead".

We have a small team stamp that I made once when we were out of town and forgot to put
our boxing stuff in the car in our rush to leave, luckily I had the clues to the boxes packed
with the laptop. So I made a small stamp that combined both our trail names from
supplies we picked up at a local art store. We now use this stamp for small logbooks, and
when we go on a hike without planning on doing any letterboxing.

Cheers,
