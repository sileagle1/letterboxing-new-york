From: dagonell2001 | Date: 2004-11-15 16:31:33 UTC
What surprized me was *how far* from out of town people came! I
thought that with an hour-and-a-half drive, I'd be the furthest
travelled. I wasn't even close. Some people drove all day Friday
just to come to what I thought would be a small local event.

One woman asked who I was. I was wearing a cavalier hat, eyepatch and
a sword at my hip because I thought it would be more fun than a
nametag. I invited her to guess. Dagonell the Pirate? I confirmed
it. "Oh yes, we got your sit-rep earlier this week." Which told me
who she was, because I had only sent out one sit-rep that week... to
Wanda and Pete! Wanda logged her 6,000th find this weekend.
Appropriately enough, #6000 was titled "Where's Wanda?" :D
-- Dagonell
