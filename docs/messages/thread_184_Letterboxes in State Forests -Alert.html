Letterboxes in State Forests -Alert

From: Jen Z | Date: 2005-06-15 18:56:23 UTC
Hey LbCNY'ers,
 
For anyone in New York who has letterboxes hidden in state forests...  please be aware that the DEC, New York State Department of Environmental Consveration (cough cough, Judas, cough cough) is planning to lease out portions of state forests/parks to oil drilling and natural gas exploration. First on the list for this "exploration" is Bear Swamp State Forest just south of Skaneateles (where I happened to plant my first series of letterboxes).
 
If you have letterboxes hidden in state forests, or were planning to HIDE letterboxes in state forests, you might want to hold off. Hopefully this abominable idea will be shut down!  Not just for the sake of letterboxing, hiking, biking, riding, birdwatching, and other outdoor pursuits at this Bear Swamp (and other forests), but for the health and prosperity of State Forests in general!!!
 
Details:

http://www.auburnpub.com/articles/2005/06/15/news/news04.txt

The text article (not online) says the DEC is holding informational meetings on June 27 and 28 in Cortland and Elmira to let the public know about this plan.  I'll be keeping my ears peeled and if need be, will pull my letterboxes (and, probably chain myself to the biggest tree I can find while holding a picket sign).
