Hi,

 

The Alphabet Bandits (www.alphabetbandits.org) and Rabbits Friends & Relations are hosting the 1st Annual Hudson Valley Letterbox Gathering

 

When:  14 May 2005

Where: Bowdoin Park Wappingers Falls, NY

Time: Noon – until Dusk

Boxes available:  IN Park: permanent 14, temporary 9

                         Within 20 minutes of the park about 10

Theme: Spring is HERE!

 

Updated Directions!!!  (sorry noticed a typo)

 

From the East, South and West, all roads lead to I-84!  Take I-84 to NY exit 11 (Route 9D/Wappingers/Beacon).  Head north on 9D towards Wappingers Falls.  Make a LEFT onto SR28 (look for the big Pink and Green Barn on the corner).  Take SR28 to the end (there used to be a stop sign here, which might be replaced by then)  Make a right on to Main St/Channingville Road.  Past the firehouse and the convenience store make a left on to Sheafe Road. Go approx a 1/2 mile and make a left into the park.  Follow signs to pavilion #4

From the North take SR9 South through Poughkeepsie after the Saturn dealership, look for green and white "Bowdoin Park 3 ->" sign on the right.  Make a right onto Sheafe road and follow to Park.
