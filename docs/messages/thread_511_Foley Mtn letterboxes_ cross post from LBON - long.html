Foley Mtn letterboxes, cross post from LBON - long!

From: norastaa | Date: 2006-05-06 12:10:01 UTC
Hi all, I met with Bond Strand today, the supervisor at Foley Mtn. As
you may recall there were some issues about LB's and geocaches in the
park. Guitarzan and I hiked to all the boxes last weekend to discover
3 or the 11 were missing:
Dancing Bear (mine) Pain Au Chocolat (CraftyMouse) and FuzzyWuzzy
Fishbones (Nickleplate). Bond had the first 2 which had been found
strewn on the trail, no bits missing and a HH in tact as well. Today
Bond wanted to see where all the boxes were so I did the hike again
(about 2 hrs!) to show him the locations. All of the hiding spots are
quite close to or right beside a trail and he had no problem with any
of them with the exception of Sheryl Crow which is under some
potentially dangerous dead branches. So we chose a new spot, he will
in fact have his tree crew trim the tree and create a log pile to just
the hide the box! He liked the creative aspect of letterboxing and
appreciated our willingness to cooperate with park staff. As well he
asked if we might do a workshop and create some LB's for the summer
camp kids, he will be looking for a theme related to park flora or fauna.
As Hoover mentioned a policy is being created to address geocaching
and letterboxing, Bond stated he sees the 2 hobbies as being quite
different. He particularly liked the location of Crafty's box which I
had hidden under the observation deck, stating that having a box so
accessible is good for the nonhikers. I concurred but did not replace
it there as someone had scratched a big X on the deck above the hiding
spot! Bond said he would get the X sanded off so that the spot would
not attract attention.... Crafty, I'll let you decide. I can rehide
your box elsewhere and replace it with one of my own so that there
would still be a very accessible box under the deck.
He has asked that no more boxes be hidden on Foley for now so would
everyone kindly remove the "BYOB" from you clues. There are other
nice spots in Westport to hide boxes, we can continue to make this
pretty place a real boxing destination without adding more boxes to
the park. There will still be 10 active boxes in the park. As well
please be
sure to mention the fee in your clue ($5 per car) and no night boxing
is permitted.
Would you please let me know when you have updated your clues...
Thanks! Norasta
ps. I have emailed Nickleplate a couple of times, no response. Their
box is defintitely gone, anybody know how to reach them?
