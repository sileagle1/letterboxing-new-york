Hudson Valley Fall Mini-gather

From: AC | Date: 2005-10-14 19:45:40 UTC

Ok… we did the winter thing with the Cookie Caper… The spring thing with Spring is HERE! And the Summer thing with Fear Faketor… Lest Fall feel left out, The Alphabet Bandits are hosting a mini-gather and hike up Mount Beacon on Sunday October 23rd.  If you’d like more info, check out AtlasQuest or e-mail me directly.

 

Mt. Beacon is a strenuous hike, rising 1,685ft in just over 1 mile.  The Baby Bandits have done it as young as 3 years old with only one needing a little assistance (namely walking while asleep for the first 1/3 of the way back down then spending the rest of it on my back)

 

This is a B.Y.O.L.T.P. (Bring Your Own Letterbox To Plant) Event.  The Bandits will be planting 2 boxes and welcome any submissions (if you can’t make it) as long as they are related to fall or the seasons.

 

Hope to hear from youse gize soon,
