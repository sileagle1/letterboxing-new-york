From: Paul Gonyea | Date: 2004-08-24 01:05:52 UTC
Misdeescrew is a crew made of "Place of Nettle" folks (adults + young folks)
who have now started planting boxes and taking up where I left off... one of
them is my niece, Deidre, or "Dee" -- but there's also Misty (so there's a
play on names here). As co-moderators, all they have to do is get folks
invited to the group, and make everyone feel welcome... If anyone has a
question, or needs some help, we'll find answers for them.

I have some letterboxing friends here in California with LOTS of experience
who I can tap into. One of them is mentioned in the Time magazine article.
His name is Ryan, and his famous stamp is of a turtle with hiking boots on -
he's well-known by the letterboxing community. I bounce ideas for some of my
more unusual boxes off of him (such as my underwater box). He'd be happy to
help anyone in this group, too.

As anyone in the area plants boxes, please post a message about the new box
here for this group. If you go hunting for boxes in New York, (even as far
away as Buffalo, Albany, Lake Placid, the Catskills or ??) tell the group
about your experiences.

So, maybe the folks in Misdeescrew would like to start by posting a message
about their Spy Island hunt and share it with others... then also let
everyone know a little bit about your latest box, Okay?

(Uncle) Paul in SF
