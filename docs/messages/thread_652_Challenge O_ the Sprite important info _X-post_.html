Challenge O' the Sprite important info (X-post)

From: Daina | Date: 2007-02-03 19:19:44 UTC
I know not all of you read the AQ boards, so here's the final update
on this!

Just to make sure everyone's on the same page, the Challenge O' the
Sprite (otherwise known as the Megastore Challenge) begins this
Monday! This challenge is in homage to TurtleMcQ's Challenge O' the
Sprite boxes that were placed inside a Wegman's grocery store, most of
which remained until the day she retired them. The idea is to plant a
box inside a store to see whose can last the longest. If you plan on
being a part of it, all you have to do is:

1. Plant a box in a megastore. That means plant your box inside one of
those big superstores that has a place to eat inside it (Super
Walmart, Super K, your local big grocery that has its own buffet area
with places to sit and eat, etc.). Malls count, too, but you must hide
it in the mall itself, not one of the stores.

2. List the box on AQ with the title "Challenge O' the Sprite: Your
Box Name Here". This will let us know to include you when we search
for boxes. If it doesn't have the title in it, we won't be looking for
it. E-mailing us to tell us to look for it will NOT help...neither one
of us can remember anything past times to eat and times to sleep! ;-)

3. Keep track of your box. The last box to be muggled will win the
extra special Challenge O' the Sprite stamp and the admiration of all
your peers (such as we are!).

The Highlander and I are going to plant Wally World today, so you
still have time. Once we post the official list on Monday, the
gauntlet will have officially been laid down, so get out there and do
some sneakin' around your local megastore!

The S & H
