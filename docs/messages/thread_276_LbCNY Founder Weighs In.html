LbCNY Founder Weighs In

From: Paul | Date: 2005-08-17 22:37:38 UTC
My intention in starting LbCNY was not to slice up NY into areas, but
rather to stimulate letterboxing in an area that needed some more
action. From the beginning, I invited anyone who letterboxed not only
in Central NY, but New York State as a whole.

If folks decided to combine the 2 Yahoo groups, WNY & CNY, and simply
make one big LbNYS, I'd still be happy. It's not about how you slice
the cake, it's about making sure everyone has some. Of course, we
haven't heard from the NYC/Long Island folks, so that might be
premature.

In California, we have 2 groups, one for Northern CA and one for
Southern CA. The 2 groups do communicate with each other and some
folks belong to both.

Whatever is going to work best for communicating about new boxes,
issues, events, and people is what should happen.

Paul in SF

I don't think the actual name of the group was the only thing that made
it seem like a separate thing. Again, the actual distance for most of
us made it seem necessary to have a place to share Western NY info. Now

The only downstate folks I know are Missmoon & Talking Turtle (who's
got a whole group of folks that we met at Fear Faketor). I'm hoping
they'll throw in their two cents, too.
