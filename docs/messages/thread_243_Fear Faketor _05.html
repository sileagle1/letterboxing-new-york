Fear Faketor '05

From: AC | Date: 2005-07-28 23:34:58 UTC

Despite a number of mishaps, Fear Faketor ’05 is here…

 

When:

Camping Friday 7/29 – Sunday 7/31/2005

Day Gather Saturday 7/30/2005 11:00 AM – 7:00 PM

 

Where:

Wilcox Park, Stanfordville, NY

Taconic Parkway to State Route 199E.  Park is 1 mile east of Taconic.  Taconic can be accessed from the North by I90 and from the South by I84

 

What:

Fear Factor style challenges and lot of boxing… There will be 10 permanent and over 25 day only boxes, including a special appearance of Boch’s Circus and a rousing round of the Clue exchange game.  Between Boxes and exchanges, there will be over 150 stamps to be had!

 

Swimming on a sand beach, row boating, paddle boating, fishing, miniature golf.  Hamburgers and hot dogs are on the menu along with pot luck sides!

 

And for Campers only…. Night Boxing!!!!

 

Hope you can make it!

 

TalkingTurtle and Kreepy Kelli
