Cootie Alert!

From: Lock Wench | Date: 2006-08-22 15:33:07 UTC
Word has it that the Cootie/HH King, Celtic Quinn (CQ to some of
you), has packed many many of his minions for this weekend's WNY
gathering at Darien Lake State Park. If you are attending either the
day event and/or the sleepover event (with cool nighttime boxing!)
be very very careful with your bags, backpacks and/or open jacket
pockets. I can speak from experience when I say.....CQ and his
siblings are relentless and utterly stealth when planting these
things. No one is safe!

Heck, I even returned from the Kingston, Ontario gathering (a
gathering where no Quinn was present) and unpacked my suitcase only
to find a grinning cootie made by Celtic Quinn under my underwear!
Don't even ask me how it got there! I DON'T WANT TO KNOW. Just
remember, you have been warned!

Seriously folks...if you haven't been to a gather yet, this is
definately the gather for you. The hosts have planned gathers
before, and they are always fun and welcoming. A perfect place for
newbies as this gather is always well attended but not so
overwhelmingly huge to make you feel lost. They always come up with
clever new games and boxes but the real joy is the spirit of
friendship exhibited by Sprite, the Highlander, and the Quinn
Clan..as well as the rest of the growing WNY boxing group.

It's not too late to come!Check out all the details here:
www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=102
