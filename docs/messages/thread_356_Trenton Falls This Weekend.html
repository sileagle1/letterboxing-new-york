Trenton Falls This Weekend

From: Cyndie | Date: 2005-09-28 15:36:39 UTC
I know this is horribly short notice, but I want people to know that
the Trenton Falls gorge will be open this weekend (Oct 1 and 2nd) and
also Oct. 8 and 9th. This amazing series of waterfalls, canyons and
gorges is only open to the public for 2 weekends in the spring, and 2
weekends in the fall, as the area is now owned by a hydroelectric
company.

I have a box located in a public area close to the access road and
Paul in SF has a box in a nearby town as well. I will be hiking the
gorge trail this Saturday, starting at 10 am. The trail is open form
9 am to 5 pm and takes about 60-90 minutes to fully appreciate. If
anyone would like to join me, you can meet me in the parking area by
the scenic trail entrance at 10 am. I will be starting the trail at
10:30 and then having lunch nearby (place to be determined).

It would be a great time to do exchanges, do some hiking/walking and
photography and enjoy this amazing natural resource before it is
closed to the public again until spring.

For pictures of this amazing place...check out the following:

http://www.mcz.harvard.edu/Departments/InvertPaleo/Trenton/Intro/Geolo
gyPage/Geologic%20Setting/geomorph_falls.htm

information, check out:
