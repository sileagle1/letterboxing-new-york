Nice logbooks

From: knitica | Date: 2006-09-16 14:51:16 UTC
Moleskin (who make those great journals some folks use as their
logbook) also makes a three pack of smaller blank books in the same
pocket size. I thought these would make great logbooks to plant in
boxes-blank pages, nice thick paper, and they could be cut in half for
smaller boxes. They seem a little harder to find than the rest of the
moleskin line, but they are sold at both Borders and Waldenbooks, the
two of which now share a no charge rewards card that looks like a
pretty nice deal. I think it was a little under $6 for the three
pack, so maybe a little pricy, but a really nice journal to save from
your box, and much more cost effective if you do cut in half.

Anyway, I saw them a while ago but my friend couldn't remember for
sure where he bought them, so now that I found them I thought I'd pass
on the good info. I have a heck of a time finding decent blank
books. For some of my smaller boxes I've just been making them.
