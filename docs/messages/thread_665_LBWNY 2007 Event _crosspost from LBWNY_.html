LBWNY 2007 Event (crosspost from LBWNY)

From: Daina | Date: 2007-05-25 02:45:58 UTC
Just a reminder to all you local folks, the Western New York gathering
is coming up as the last holler of summer (and as representatives of
said region, you are BOUND BY HONOR to attend, or at least send an
envoy in your stead!) :-) We'll be in Orchard Park (Chestnut Ridge, to
be exact) and our theme this year is "(LB)WNY is ON THE AIR!". We
intend to do a standard day tripper's Saturday event from 10-5, but
also plan to have night boxing again and hopefully camping from Friday
night to Sunday at one of the closer campgrounds.

We'd REALLY like to seed this park with boxes enough to make this trip
as worthwhile as our past gatherings have been (okay, and to try and
meet or beat the Stone Mountain record!), so we'd love to get box
donations. Any stamps you'd like to create that deal with music of any
kind are great, so long as we can get them by the beginning of August
(gives us enough time to plant them). Contact us offlist and we'll
give the address to send them to.

Thanks in advance and hope to see many more of you there!

The Sprite, the Highlander and this year's local host in OP, Chadams

Did you repost on accident, Shirl? :-)

For anyone needing directions to the park, the address is 6121
Chestnut Ridge Road, Orchard Park, NY 14127 If you put that into Yahoo
Maps, Mapquest or Google Maps, that will let you do driving directions
from where you live to the park. Shirl, I put in directions from
Washington Hunt Elementary in Lockport to the park as an example:
http://maps.yahoo.com/flash/#mvt=m&q2=6121+CHESTNUT+RIDGE+RD%2C+Orchard+Park%2C+NY%2C+14127&gid1=11702787&q1=50+Rogers+Avenue%2C+Lockport%2C+NY+14094&trf=0&lon=-78.673246⪫=43.177376&mag=3


By the way, you can also use the map search on the event page to do
this and it will already have the park's address in for you! It's
right in the location box near the top of the page:
http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=221
