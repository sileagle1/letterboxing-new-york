2 weeks in Manhattan!

From: Paul | Date: 2005-09-02 03:31:17 UTC
I'll be in Manhattan on vacation/sabbatical for 2 whole weeks at the
end of the month. I'm REALLY curious to see what kinds of ULTRA-
URBAN boxes survive in Gotham City. I believe there are about 18 of
them listed in Manhattan alone. I fully understand the issues around
urban planting -- mine here in SF often vanish -- but with that
population intensity -- Yikes!

That's one place where Lock Wench's super micro-teeny-tiny-itsy-
bitsy magnetic dot boxes (as for Leprechaun's Revenge)would work
really well.

Has anyone on LbNY found these NYC boxes? I just invited the
planter, Miss Moon, to join LbNY, BTW.

I feel a strong challenge to plant one or two in The Big Apple
myself -- and I won't be surprised if MisDeesCrew hides one down
there next week when they go.

Oh, and that crafty Lisascenic has planted 2 boxes across the Bay in
a rose garden in Oakland that she says are in honor of William
Shakespeare, Gertrude Stein, and (blush) yours truly, Paul in SF.
That's another piece of my sabbatical...

Paul in SF
