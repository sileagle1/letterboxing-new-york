Hi All,

 

Its me… Talking Turtle again… sorry to BOMBARD the lists with e-mails, but I have e-mails coming in left and right about the Clue Exchange Game.   So I thought it best to address everyone’s questions at once

 

Please bear with me, as I have never hosted the Clue Exchange game … well to be quite honest… I’ve never actually played the board game Clue either…. Hey… I was an only kid… board games just aren’t as fun by yourself, although I have played all 4 colors of Sorry! By myself… what a case of self loathing that creates LOL 

 

#1  While I will have the pieces carved for the original Clue Exchange game played at the gathering last May, those are reserved for those who arrive and decide they want to play that day, and therefore don’t have a piece.

 

#2  The theme of the pieces if you decide to carve one or more, will be.. what else? Fear Factor and Letterboxing.  To give you an example, So far we have:

 

Suspect                        Weapon                        Location                Carver

Apolonia Trailenheffer     Cow entrail rope             Out-house             Talking Turtle

Rocky M. Ountainside    Poisonous snail darts     Abandoned Well     Blax

Sheleda B. Foolishly      Killer bee hive                Up a tree                Gizmo

 

#3  It would be best (most convenient) for the Clue Master to have all the stamps prior to the gathering, as there is some preparation involved. I’ll have a LOT to do that Saturday morning. 

 

#4 If anyone would like to take over the duties of Clue Master please do not hesitate to let me know.  Scarab and I will coach you.

 

#5 For those who have never played clue, the basic concept is that from a blind pool, a suspect, weapon and location are chosen.  The remaining stamps are then shuffled again and passed out to those playing.  The object is to exchange stamps with everyone playing until you can figure out which ones are missing from the original pool.  Once you think you know the correct missing pieces, you DISCREETLY whisper it to the Clue Master.  The winners will not be announced until 1st, 2nd and 3rd places have been confirmed.  Prizes will be awarded then.

 

That covers all the questions that have come up so far this afternoon.

 

Hope to see you all there!
