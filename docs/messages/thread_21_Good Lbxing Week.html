Good Lbxing Week

From: MTnestRobin | Date: 2004-10-03 03:53:54 UTC
Hi All,

Wow! I'm excited to see our members have doubled in less than a week!
Yeah...now lets do that with letterboxes too! Some of you already
have a good head start...I gotta get crackin'!

This has been a good letterbox related week for me! Besides the
pleasure of seeing this list grow, earlier this week I met up for an
exchange with Seth Mandeville of team Dynamic Duo from RI! (Ya gotta
love these exchanges...its refreshing to unleash a little enthusiasm
and not be regarded as escapees from the asylum!) Seth was here in
the North Country doing some salmon fishing and letterboxing...taking
his brother and dad on their first LB experience to my Lake Ontario
Dune Trail series! Score one new letterboxer and one, at the very
least, sympathetic observer... which as it turned out, was better
than the fishing results!

And then to round out the week__ Team Frick'n'Frack went out
letterboxing today despite the rain! We did the Sterling Nature
Center Series(5) and "His Dark Materials" Series(6) in the Fair
Haven/Oswego area. If you haven't been there... both of these series
are well done with very nice stamps!

Upping my PFX thanks to many of you!
MTnestRobin
