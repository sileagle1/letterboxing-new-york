Hey all ...

Making the semi-annual pilgramage to Western New York this coming
weekend. Five days over the Memorial Day holiday.

Unfortunately, I won't have too much time for letterboxing this
trip, as I am bringing my GF back for the first time. I'll end up
playing the tourist host mostly.

That's OK, of course, as I have many of the boxes already that are
in the Rochester area ... oh wait, you mean there 4,568 new ones?
Ah, yes, I see that ... GEEZ, YOU GUYS HAVE BEEN BUSY!!

Well, I guess I won't clear Ontario and Monroe counties this time
either. Matter-of-fact, I'm not even going to get close. I mean
there's 48 boxes in the Just Playin' Around WNY series alone!
(Speaking of which, the clues his boxes with designated an * will be
pulled by October 2005, but no boxes in the clues are designated
with an * -- are they all there?)

I was hoping to plant another box in the area so that my "You Press
the Button, We Do the Rest" box wasn't alone, but I never got one
prepared. Next time!

AND ... if anyone wants to meet for a quick exchange, let me know.
I'll have my cell phone with me ... feel free to call. Send me an e-
mail message today and I'll send you back my cell phone number (I'll
check e-mails when I arrive there tonight).


And stop planting letterboxes!!! At least until I can catch up ...

LOL.
