Now, c'mon, Miss Moon...don't tell me that living in the city has made
you soft!! *wink*

Actually, some of my best times living in NY have been during
blizzards. People are so nice and just go out of their way to shovel
your walk, push out your car, get you to the pharmacy on a snowmobile
(really!) and play games by the fireplace and under candle light when
the power goes off. Of course there was the blizzard of Christmas
2002..when I was trying desperately to get to my dying mother in a
blinding snowstorm and got within 4 miles of her house before I had to
take refuge in a motel just off the exit. My kids and I had a dinner
of Christmas cookies (that I had made for gifts) and soda from the
machine..while I popped open the bottles of wine I had bought as gifts
and shared them with our fellow stranded motel guests.... Two days
later I made it to Mom's..and she died 3 days later.

I just want safe driving conditions for everyone, of course..
