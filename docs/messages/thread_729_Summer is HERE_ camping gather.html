Hi There!

 

Happy New Year!!!  

 

Just a reminder that we have begun making reservations for the bi-annual Hudson Valley Letterboxing Gather and camping fees are due.  I’ll be making reservations by the end of January.  There are only 4 tent and 7 trailer sites left!   This year the featured season is Summer.  The Spring was held in May of 2005 and fall was in October of 2007.  There are only 4 sites left! Camping check-in is after 3pm Friday, check out by noon Sunday.

 

The day gather will be held onsite Saturday 1 August at Wilcox Park in Stanfordville, NY about 5 minutes off the Taconic State Parkway.  30 minutes south of I90 and 30 minutes north of I84. 

 

This park has 7 miles of hiking trails, a life guard manned beach, boating, fishing, miniature golf and lots of boxes.  In addition to the (approx 20) Summer boxes, the Top Ten Phobias are here.

 

If you are interested in carving a summer themed stamp for the event contact rabbitrelations AT atlasquest.com

 

Hope to hear from you soon,

Talking Turtle
