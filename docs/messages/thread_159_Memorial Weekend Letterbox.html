Memorial Weekend Letterbox

From: MTnestRobin | Date: 2005-05-28 01:37:38 UTC
I just placed a letterbox that will only be available on the three
major holiday weekends!It's called The Coffee Stop!

If you happen to be travelling up I-81 over the weekend, stop in at
the
Coffee Stop set up at the rest stop between Mannsville(exit 39)and
Pierrepont Manor(exit 40). Check out the generator powering the
operation and look behind the big black toolbox(on the woods side)and
you will see the letterbox sitting there wrapped in a black bag. Most
of the volunteers are accustom to the occasional interest in the
generator (and fire truck), so pretend to have some! The volunteers
do
not know about the letterbox, but MtnMan (Mick) does and he runs the
coffee stop!

Clues to this letterbox will be posted on LbNA later this weekend! I
had to wait until the coffee stop was set up to know where to place
the
box so it would be in the same place no matter which side the coffee
stop was on. Consider these the clues until then!

There is another letterbox in each of these rest stops, see Coming
and
Going! Have a safe weekend!
