WOW! Last year at this time, boxes were few and far between... and
now, there's some darn good LB'ing to be had in CNY!

Today, we nabbed Moss Island, which I enjoyed so much that I left a
new box just 1 or 2 feet directly above the Moss Island box, to
entice people to come or return to Moss Island. Two boxes so close to
each other -- who can resist. I'll post my directions later.

Then we went on to Herkimer Home, followed by Skunk Bridge in
Herkimer, then finished with The Great Swimming Hole Caper at Trenton
Falls. Four boxes for 3 kids and 3 adults!

On the way home, I planted one more box in Barneveld, so those
directions will be posted soon, too!

Beautiful day!

Tomorrow, I have the use of my niece's car to find the new boxes my
other niece and friend planted -- plus, plant one or two more &
replace the missing Oriskany Namesake boxes!

On Saturday, I want to have printed copies of the clues for the
nearest boxes available, as well.
