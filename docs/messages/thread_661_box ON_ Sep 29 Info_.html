Alafair asked if I could post the gathering info here for the Sep 29
Box ON! gathering, so will post it here for everyones benefit.

If you have any specific questions, I'll be happy to address them...

What: Box ON! letterbox gathering
Where: Victoria Park, Belleville, ON (approx 80 Mi West of the 1000
Islands bridge)
When: 29 Sep 07 from 9:30 to 5:30
Who: all letterboxers and families/sig others welcome. Pets Ok on a
leash (Dog Park fairly nearby as well)
Cost: donations greatfully accepted
Potluck lunch. Should have a BBQ or 2 available as well

There are currently about 75 LBs in the Kingston - Brighton
corridor. That should swell to over 100 by the gathering.

This promises to be a more intimate (read smaller) gathering than
many we've been to in CA and NY, so there will be ample opportunity
to exchange, swap some lies and do some serious boxing.

As seems to be becoming a bit of a tradition, there'll probably be a
pre- and post gather gathering as well (anyone remember the
Batchelor Party in Kingston??) More to follow.

For those that would like to participate , but can't attend, we'd be
happy to place any donated boxes. The theme is Ontario, but we're
pretty flexible.

Cheers, Jiggs
