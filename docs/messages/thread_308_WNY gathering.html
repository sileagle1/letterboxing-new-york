WNY gathering

From: Cyndie | Date: 2005-08-28 18:24:24 UTC
I was so sorry to hear of Lucy's injury. I will say a novena for her
this evening.

But as for the rest of the gathering...it was GREAT! The park was
gorgeous and I will need to come back and explore more of it's 965
acres. I saw deer, rabbits and blue herons. My trail mates (Catbead,
Jiggs and Dagonell) were wonderful company and expert boxers. We had
the boxes found, opened, stamped, and rehidden very well in record
time. Because of their expertise, I was able to add 30+ stampts to
my collection. I even hit the F-100 mark on Craftymouse's KISS MY
ACE. A truly wonderful carving.

Daina and Kevin were the consummate hosts. The booklets were so handy
and I appreciate all the time that went into making them. It was a
wonderful feast (even if I did forget MY dish at home...Hungarian
Goulash for 20, anyone?) and got to meet so many wonderful people.

Catbead and I tried to catch a few more boxes on the way out before
it got TOO dark....( ok ok...I did get a COUPLE in complete
blackness) but by then we needed to head back. Meeting Tamsyn, Tony
and Phynstar at the Thruway stop was a nice surprise. Hitting a
raccoon at 75 mph was not. :(

I would like to thank everyone who donated items and boxes and great
food and conversation. And an especially big thank you to
Sprite...you did a FABULOUS job!!
