Albany, Buffalo, Alfred, and NYC boxers needed...

From: johnoballou | Date: 2006-02-24 19:22:39 UTC
Hey, I was sitting here in my office today at the University of Rochester, and I started to
hear our Carillon being played (for you who don't know, this is a set of more than 23
bells). It chimes every fifteen minutes, but a person can actually play songs in it too, and
this doesn't happen everyday here so it caught my attention.

Anyway, upon looking at info about our Hopeman Memorial Carrilon (50 bells) in Rush
Rhees Library, I learned there are only 7 carillons in New York. (is you have less then 23,
you are a set of chimes, like at Cornell in Ithaca).

So, here's my idea. I would like to do a letterbox series, a letterbox at each of the seven
carillons in New York State. I can do the Rochester one, but I need others to carve and
sponsor boxes at the others! Here's the list...

Alfred - Alfred Univesity - Davis Memorial Carillon - 47 bells

Williamsville - Niederlander Carillon - Calvary Episcopal Church - 44 bells

Albany - Albany City Hall - 49 bells

NYC - Riverside Church - Laura Spelman Rockefeller Memorial Carillon - 74 bells (I already
have a boxer for this one)

NYC - St. Martin's Church - 42 bells

NYC - St. Thomas Church - 26 bells

Any takers?
