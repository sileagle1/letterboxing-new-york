From: Daina | Date: 2006-03-11 15:27:47 UTC
I know most of the NY boxers were invested in this process with us,
so I'm reposting this from AQ:

Well, I know everyone was really excited for us, but it looks like
this particular version of the baby project is going to have to go by
the wayside. The doctors are telling us the cells I had started are
actually regressing instead of developing and we have yet to see a
heartbeat, so they'll be doing a procedure next week to clear out
everything and start over.

I was a little upset at first, but what I have are cells that had not
matured and are in danger of doing harm to my system now. In that
light, this is more of a new beginning than an ending, and as the
Highlander puts it "It's magic waiting to happen, so it hasn't really
left...it's just biding its time until we get the right form for it."

So, we're okay, but maybe we should all hold off on any baby names,
boxes, events or anything for a bit (just so we aren't sponging off
everyone's overwhelming kindness without an actual kiddo to show for
it!).

Regardless, thanks to everyone for your support...I don't think I
realized how important the boxing community was until now. Outside of
family and our jobs (who HAVE to know), you're the only group we've
shared this information with. :-)

The (thankful for the support of good friends, seen and unseen)
