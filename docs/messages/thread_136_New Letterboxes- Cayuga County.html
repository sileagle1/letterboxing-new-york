New Letterboxes- Cayuga County

From: Jen Z | Date: 2005-05-09 13:00:34 UTC
Hi Everyone,
 
I just planted my first letterboxes this weekend! :o) Wow, was that fun! The series is called "Spring has Sprung" (carved last month in the fits of late-winter blahs) and is hidden at Bear Swamp State Forest, a little gem on the western shore of Skaneateles Lake in Cayuga County.
 
Clues can be found at:
 
http://www.letterboxing.org/BoxView.php?boxnum=14941&boxname=Spring_Has_Sprung
 
We had a wonderful time although planting the boxes and coming up with clues took much more mental energy than I thought! :o) My greyhounds Lucy and Clifford accompanied me and had a blast (although they thought it was a cardinal sin to leave the trail... unless a carcass or pee-smell was involved of course!). :o)
 
A trip to Micheal's ensued for more carving material... this stuff is addicting! :o)
 
~"Trailhoundz"

Re: New Letterboxes- Cayuga County

From: Jen Z | Date: 2005-05-16 16:24:46 UTC
I'm very sad to report that Box #2 of my first Letterbox carvings I planted just 2 weeks ago ("Spring Has Sprung" at Bear Swamp State Forest in Sempronius, NY)  has been stolen. I've now had 2 confirmations from 2 different Letterboxers.  "Kit Kat" informed me that there was a very, VERY rare Purple Trillium growing right in front of the tree where I hid it, and someone might have gone off the trail to look at the flower and seen the cairn of rocks tucked behind the tree and dug it up. :o( 
 
I'll make a note on my clues at www.Letterboxing.org so folks will know it's gone, but wanted to let the list know too.
 
Man, this stinks!!  :o( 
 
"Trailhoundz"
~Jen with ex-racing greyhounds Lucy and Clifford
