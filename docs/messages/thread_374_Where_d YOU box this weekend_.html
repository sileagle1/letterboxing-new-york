Where'd YOU box this weekend?

From: Daina | Date: 2005-10-16 02:09:36 UTC
Never think to post an update, but today was so nice, we finally
decided to post a little WNY action! ;-)

FINALLY had a day of boxing with just the Highlander and I! I get so
caught up asking others to box with us that I had forgotten what it
was like to just be out together doing what we love...walking and
talking in the autumn splendor while searching for boxes. The
Highlander's neck problems have abated considerably, so he finally
got to enjoy a day of hiking, too.

Rochester is really a nice place (regardless of what the Buffalo
snobs may say!). We LOVED the Urban Letterboxer's Eastman School of
Music series, one of which was another book hidden like our
Letterbook box. Unfortunately, out of the 8 boxes we tried, 3 were
missing/couldn't be found, and one just got too dark (thank goodness
for the Highlander in dark scary places at night! Still, it was a
terrific day...not too hot, not too cold, not too long or short a
walk, everything just right! I swear, we're not boxing with ANYone
else for at least a month, just so we can hang out with each other
all over again! I think we'll also start boxing earlier in the
day...I love sleeping in, but on a boxing day, it just seems a shame
to sleep away those extra hours.

Now, if I could just get my PLB going, my boxing life would be
easy! :-P

The (back in the saddle again) Sprite & the (loving the ride, but
glad to be back to the bunkhouse) Highlander
