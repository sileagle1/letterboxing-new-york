From: thespiritofmrpigeon | Date: 2005-11-16 18:32:53 UTC
Interestingly enough, the purple skull was stamped on a page
containing YOUR stamps, MTNestRobin! I admire your stamps immensely
and thus was keeping the page I used "as is". The skull definitely
appeared during the Ithaca Gathering because I spotted it in our book
as soon as we were in the car and driving away.

I admit, I think it's interesting all the talk that's transpired since
someone brought up the subject of the skull. It is a neat idea,
whatever the heck it's supposed to be. Although I can't shake the
feeling that I've been marked in some way. If it was a purple star, or
a sun, or some unknown swirly design, I'd feel better about it. But a
skull..... have I been marked for letterboxer termination?!

Craftymouse....who's paws will remain firmly on her logbook at every
gathering from now on...
