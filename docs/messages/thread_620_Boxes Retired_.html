Just a heads up, if anyone was planning on searching for the Celtic
Cross and Scottish Thistle letterboxes at St. Paul's in Oswego,
they've been retired. With the terrible wind that we've had the past
few days, I had a feeling that I needed to check on them. It was a
good thing I did because the tree that was housing the Cross had
toppled over earlier in the day. These 2 boxes have given us trouble
in the past (in the re-hiding department) and the logbook pages were
just about full anyway, so I figured replacing them elsewhere in the
cemetery would probably just be pressing my luck. LBNA and AQ have
been updated.

Thanks to all the folks who found these boxes!
