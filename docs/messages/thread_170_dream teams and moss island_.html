dream teams and moss island!

From: Lisa Lazar | Date: 2005-06-06 13:49:15 UTC
Whoo hoo! I had a great weekend. I did the "Moss Island" hike, which
was a blast!!!! Thank you Lock Wench for pointing us to this amazing
spot. I dragged along two non-letterboxing friends and I think they
are hooked.

I also found "Dream Teams" boxes in Cooperstown. I'm so impressed
that the Girl Guides actually found anywhere to hide boxes in the
hyper-manicured village of Cooperstown.

Now all I have to do is locate Paul in SF's secret swimming hole
spot.....

And finally, I've planted five boxes in the Cooperstown area. Look
for "Healing Waters" in Sharon Springs, and "Nature at the Opera" in
Cooperstown. I had some trouble posting, so they're not listed with
both AQ and LBNA at the moment. You can find both of them on AQ for
the time being.

Lisascenic
