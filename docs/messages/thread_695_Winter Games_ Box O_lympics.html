Winter Games: Box O'lympics

From: AC | Date: 2007-09-14 12:55:53 UTC

Are you ready to go for the gold? If so, you are invited to attend the 2008 Winter Games – Box O’Lympics, a weekend and day event in the Hudson Valley (Friday January 25th – Sunday  January 27th, 2008).  Join us for the weekend for a casual Friday Night get together full of good food and good friends.  Saturday event, full of winter letterboxing and outdoor games, cross country skiing, and sledding on the best sledding hill in the area (Just ask Butterfly! http://tinyurl.com/2u7phd ).  Saturday evening is reserved for “Pajama Games”,  team and individual board games!  Spend Sunday leisurely finding the area’s jewels.

 

Accommodations will be at a local winter lodge including bedrooms, a fireplace, full kitchen and baths and a HUGE great room with a fireplace  for fun and games.  Space for the weekend event is EXTREMELY limited (25).  Due to the space limitations we must limit the number of children attending the weekend event.  L  (The baby bandits won’t even be staying!) E-mail me for more details

 

The Day Event (Saturday January 26th 2008) will include a full day of:

Winter boxing, special event boxes,  limited time only boxes, 30 traditional boxes in the park alone!  Don’t like the cold? Tons of indoor boxes, some easy, some NOT so easy!

Outdoor winter games – games of skill, races, team games, sledding, cross country skiing (skis not provided).  Creative?  Sign up for the Snow Painting Contest!

Indoor games and prizes - raffles, contests, board games

Pot Luck!

 

If you like to submit a “GAME” related stamp or raffle prize, we’d love to have it! What was (is) your favorite game?  Sorry, Operation, Headache, Huskerdoo, Trivial Pursuit, Taboo, Scene it?  We’d also like Winter Games themed stamps… the Olympic rings, the luge, Figure skating… use your imagination.

 

Visit AQ to RSVP to one or both events and e-mail me for more details if you’d like to join us for the weekend!                         

 

It’s going to be a blast!

 

Talking Turtle and Kreepy Kelli

The Winter Games Box O’Lympics Weekend event is open for reservations, there are only 8 spaces left and we’re beginning to receive deposits.  If you are interested in attending, RSVP soon and I’ll send you more information.

 

Who: Anyone who wants to spend a weekend of boxing and board games with great friends!

What:  Weekend http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=473 and day events http://www.atlasquest.com/events/event.html?gEventId=472 combining boxing and board games.  There’s a great sledding hill, cross country skiing  and 5 miles of trails.  15 event specific boxes,  25 or so park specific boxes, and another 25 or so boxes within 10 miles including a number in indoor boxes (not available on Sundays)  Although I’m not a geo-cacher, there are a couple of caches nearby.  IF you’d like to give caching a try, there will be a GPS unit available for sign out so we can see how the other half lives

Where: a heated 4 season lodge in New York on the Poughkeepsie, Wappingers border. About 15 minutes north of I84

When: Friday January 25th – Sunday January 27th 2008.  Check in is Friday 5PM and checkout is anytime before Sunday 5PM. 

Why: Why not?

How much: Cost is $30 for the weekend which includes dinner Friday and breakfast Saturday and Sunday.

Theme:  Winter games, board games, outdoor games, word games, ALL kinds of games!

 

I’m now accepting carving and raffle offerings!   If you’d like to carve a stamp based either the Olympics, games or board games theme, or have something you’d like to donate for raffle, e-mail me off list.
